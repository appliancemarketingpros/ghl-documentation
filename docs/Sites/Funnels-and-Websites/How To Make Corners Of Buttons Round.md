# How To Make Corners Of Buttons Round

**Source URL:** [https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/48000986876-how-to-make-corners-of-buttons-round](https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/48000986876-how-to-make-corners-of-buttons-round)  
**Category:** Sites  
**Folder:** Funnels and Websites

---

To round the corners of buttons in the Funnel Builder: 

  


1\. Select the button element

  


2\. Go to Advanced settings

  


3\. Add a border to all sides and adjust border color if needed

  


4\. Change border radius to 100px
