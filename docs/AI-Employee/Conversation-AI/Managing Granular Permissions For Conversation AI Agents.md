# **What are Granular Permissions For Conversation AI Agents?**

  


Granular Permissions For Conversation AI Agents is a HighLevel feature that allows administrators to define exactly what team members can view, manage, or configure within Conversation AI Bots. By assigning these permissions, you ensure that only authorized roles have control over bot creation, goals, and training. This strengthens system integrity and provides flexible access management that aligns with team responsibilities.

* * *

## **Key Benefits of Granular Permissions For Conversation AI Agents**

  


  * **Security:** Safeguards bot configurations, goals, and training from unauthorized changes.  
  


  * **Role-based access:** Enable tailored team access based on roles and responsibilities.  
  

  * **Efficiency:** Streamlines workflows by hiding unnecessary or restricted options.  
  


  * **Risk reduction:** Minimizes accidental or unauthorized modifications through controlled access.

## **Permission Types Explained**

**  
**

## **1\. View & Manage Conversation AI Agents**

  


This permission controls overall management of Conversation AI Bots, including creation, editing, and deletion.

  


When enabled:  
  


  * Grants **full access** to **create, edit, and delete** Conversation AI Bots.  
  


  * Activates dependent permissions, including **View & Manage Conversation AI agent Goals, View Conversation AI agent** and **View & Manage Conversation AI agent Training.**  
  
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155051899123/original/t9_uzbIDr7ud4DWI3JmJZKXRcSsdCgM_MA.png?1755605006)  
  


When disabled:

  


  * Hides the entire **Conversation AI** Bots menu item.  
  


  * Removes access to the list view and prevents new bot creation.  
  


  * Automatically disables dependent permissions and conceals Conversation AI from AI Agents menus.  
  
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155051899169/original/BbkO7qSSJxhr0cGxMo1lgrrx7SM1Nj5MiA.png?1755605052)  
  


## **2\. View & Manage Conversation AI agent Goals**

  


This permission provides control over **editing** **bot goals** —ideal for advanced users configuring bot logic and objectives.

  


When enabled:  
  


  * Allows users to **edit** **B****ot Goals** while editing an existing bot.  
  


  * Suitable for power users configuring bot objectives and response logic.  
  
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155051899247/original/UPtGJaTHP_DyOyxCDTORthNGvu7tnLEfBA.png?1755605090)  
  


When disabled:

  


  * Hides the **Bot Goals** tab during editing.  
  


  * Users can still define goals during the creation process for form-based bots.  
  


  * Bot trial access remains available.  
  
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155051899967/original/EaTiawj6WXLGlilFBbyFZbXttE4dEUKNhQ.png?1755605501)  
  


## **3\. View Conversation AI Agent Goals**

  


This setting is for users who need to **view-only,** but not edit, configured **bot goals**.  
  


When enabled:

  


  * Allows users to **view** configured **bot goals** such as instructions and actions.  
  


  * Extends visibility to prompt details within the AI response info drawer.  
  
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155051900252/original/EmzWofTJ7JvSC24-bDD2Z0ToFSzW1mHYiQ.png?1755605642)  
  


When disabled:

  


  * Hides the **Bot Goals** tab during editing.  
  


  * Goals are only visible during form-based bot creation.  
  


  * Hides additional instructions in that context.


  


## **4\. View & Manage Conversation AI agent Training**

  


This permission controls access to **bot** **training** configurations by determining who can attach or detach a knowledge base from a bot.  
  


When enabled:

  


  * Users can attach/detach a **knowledge base** from the bot.  
  


  * Users can see which knowledge base was used for a response within the AI response drawer.  
  
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155051900290/original/-uywLXbF4GGroDJt4w0KsOCos9AsykCbzg.png?1755605695)  
  


When disabled:  
  


  * Hides the **Bot Training tab** and prevents modifications.  
  


  * Removes knowledge base references from the AI response drawer in conversations.  
  
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155051900302/original/DbPT5hj9ftGmI9elDyHy1OTJPZ161D2-cA.png?1755605711)  


  

    
    
    **Note:** If both **View & Manage Conversation AI Training** and **View & Manage Conversation AI agent Goals** are disabled, the Bot Trial feature becomes inaccessible.

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

  


**Q: What happens if I disable “View & Manage Conversation AI Agents”?**  
The entire Conversation AI Bots menu disappears, preventing creation or editing. All dependent permissions are also disabled.  
  


**Q: Can I give a user read-only access to bot goals but restrict training?**  
Yes, you can enable **View Conversation AI Agent Goals** while disabling **View & Manage Conversation AI Agent Training** for visibility without training access.  
  


**Q: If I disable “View & Manage Conversation AI Agent Goals,” can users still set goals?**  
Yes, they can still define goals during the creation of form-based bots, but not when editing existing bots.  
  


**Q: How does disabling “View & Manage Conversation AI Agent Training” affect bots?**  
It removes access to the Bot Training tab, prevents attaching/detaching knowledge bases, and hides training details from the AI response drawer.  
  


**Q: What happens to Bot Trial if goal and training permissions are both disabled?**  
The Bot Trial feature will be inaccessible if both **View & Manage Conversation AI Agent Training and View & Manage Conversation AI Agent Goals **are disabled.

  


**Q: Why can’t I see the Conversation AI Dashboard?**  
Make sure the **View Conversation AI Dashboard** permission is enabled under Roles & Permissions → AI Agents. Disabling it hides the dashboard from the Conversation AI home page and agent-level pages
