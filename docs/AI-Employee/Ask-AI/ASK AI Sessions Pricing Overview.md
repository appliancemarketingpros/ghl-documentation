This article provides examples of how Ask AI sessions are structured and how usage costs are calculated.

**Each example explains:**

  * The user request
  * How Ask AI processed the task

  * The total session cost


These examples help show how different task types, from simple content creation to multi-step workflows, can impact overall Ask AI usage.

## Example 1 — FederFlex Product Import & Currency Update

### Overview

Ask AI imported products from an external Shopify store, created the product in the location, matched the product pricing, and updated the location currency.

### User Request

Pull a list of all the products from <https://federflex.com/collections/all> and add them as products in the location. Also set the location and product currency to match.

### How Ask AI Processed the Task

Ask AI identified this as a multi-step product import and configuration task.

It reviewed the provided FederFlex collection URL, retrieved the available Shopify product data, identified one main product with multiple variants, checked the current location currency, and prepared the required actions for approval.

After approval, Ask AI created the product, added INR pricing, and updated the location currency from AED to INR.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155070675961/original/XVQ99V68YfOQs7d-26wXmAoAr1iI46Ayxg.png?1778057046)

### Actions Performed

  * Reviewed the product source URL
  * Retrieved available product information
  * **Identified the main product:** Compression Calf Sleeve — The StrideLine Collection
  * Reviewed product variants, including color and size options
  * Checked the current location currency
  * Confirmed the source product currency as INR
  * Prepared approval actions for product creation, pricing, and currency update
  * Created the product in the location
  * Added the INR product price
  * Updated the location currency from AED to INR


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155070676019/original/I_c1dvl7EPncINTUTDcsFGqpo8idQxZtFw.png?1778057065)

### Session Details

  * **Total turns:** 15
  * **Total user messages needed:** 2
  * **Rendering involved:** No image rendering; approval/action cards were used
  * **Final output:** Product created, INR price added, and location currency updated from AED to INR
  * **Total session cost:** **$1.1757**

## Example 2 — Fitness Email Template Setup

### Overview

Ask AI helped prepare a fitness coaching funnel and created follow-up email templates for the business.

### User Request

Create a complete funnel for my fitness coaching business including landing page, form, email follow-ups, and booking calendar.

### How Ask AI Processed the Task

Ask AI first collected the required business details, including the business name, coaching specialty, ideal client, program name, and discovery call preference.

After reviewing the available platform actions, Ask AI determined that direct funnel, form, and calendar creation were not available through the API for this session.

It continued with the supported action: creating email templates.

  * Identified the coaching specialty as **Strength & Muscle Building**

## Example 3 — Multi-Channel Google I/O Campaign Preparation

### Overview

Ask AI prepared a complete multi-channel event marketing campaign for the Google I/O 2026 Watch Party & AI Recap Session.

### User Request

Launch a complete multi-channel campaign for my upcoming webinar/event across email, SMS, and social media, and prepare everything for approval.

### Event Details

  * **Title:** Google I/O 2026 Watch Party & AI Recap Session
  * **Topic:** Key AI, Gemini, Android, and developer updates from Google I/O 2026
  * **Date & Time:** May 19, 2026 at 7:00 PM IST
  * **Target Audience:** Developers, SaaS founders, agency owners, marketers, and AI enthusiasts
  * **Registration Link:** <https://io.google/2026/register/>

### How Ask AI Processed the Task

Ask AI identified this as a large multi-channel campaign setup request.

It reviewed the event details, retrieved the brand board, generated an event banner, and prepared campaign assets across email, SMS, WhatsApp, LinkedIn, Facebook, and Instagram.

Ask AI also checked platform readiness. SMS-capable numbers were available, but social accounts were not connected and the WhatsApp subscription was not fully active.

## Key Takeaways

  * Ask AI session costs vary based on task complexity, number of steps, and features used.
  * Simple content generation or template creation tasks typically have lower costs.
  * Multi-step workflows may require more processing and result in higher usage.
  * Rendering actions such as image generation or visual asset creation can increase session cost.
  * Approval-based workflows help ensure actions are reviewed before anything is created, updated, published, or sent.
