# **Understanding Ask AI Session Costs**  
  


Ask AI sessions consist of one or more conversational turns where the AI plans, reasons, generates content, performs supported platform actions, and requests approvals when necessary. Every session is measured based on the work performed rather than simply the number of prompts submitted.  
  


A typical session may include:  
  


  * Understanding your request  
  

  * Gathering additional information through Guided Questions  
  

  * Retrieving platform or external data  
  

  * Planning multiple steps  
  

  * Creating content or assets  
  

  * Rendering structured artifacts such as tables or approval cards  
  

  * Executing supported platform actions  
  

  * Requesting approval before making changes  
  


More complex workflows generally require additional processing and therefore result in higher usage costs.

## **Ask AI Pricing Overview**  
  


Understanding the available pricing models helps you choose the option that best fits your usage patterns.  
  


### **AI Employee Plan**  
  


The AI Employee subscription includes Ask AI as part of the complete AI Employee offering, providing unlimited access under the plan’s included usage and features.  
  


### **Pay Per Use**  
  


Ask AI is also available through Pay Per Use pricing, allowing customers to pay only for the AI resources consumed during each session.  
  


Session costs depend on factors such as:  
  


  * Number of reasoning steps  
  

  * Workflow complexity  
  

  * Content generation  
  

  * Images or other generated media  
  

  * Charts, Mermaid diagrams, and structured artifacts  
  

  * External data retrieval  
  

  * Platform actions  
  

  * Approval workflows  
  


Simple drafting sessions typically cost significantly less than multi-step automations involving asset generation and platform updates.

## **Product Import & Currency Update**

  
Product imports often involve multiple coordinated actions across inventory, pricing, and location settings. This example demonstrates how Ask AI retrieves external product data, prepares approvals, and completes supported actions.  
  


## **Overview**  
  


Ask AI imported products from an external Shopify store, created the product inside the location, matched the product pricing, and updated the location currency.  
  


**User Request**

  
Pull a list of all the products from https://federflex.com/collections/all and add them as products in the location. Also set the location and product currency to match.

Approval cards for product creation, pricing, and location currency updates.  
  


###   
**How Ask AI Processed the Task**

  


Ask AI:  
  


  * Retrieved the Shopify product catalog  
  

  * Identified one primary product with multiple variants  
  

  * Reviewed color and size options  
  

  * Verified pricing  
  

  * Checked the current location currency

  * Created the product after approval

## **What Affects Ask AI Session Costs?**  
  


Ask AI usage is based on the work performed during a session rather than the number of prompts alone. Sessions become more resource-intensive as additional reasoning, content generation, or platform actions are required.

Several factors can increase overall session cost, including:  
  


  * Multi-step reasoning and planning  
  

  * Guided Questions and follow-up interactions  
  

  * Platform integrations  
  

  * Product imports  
  

  * Email or marketing campaign creation
  *   

  * Approval workflows  
  

  * Charts, tables, and Mermaid diagrams  
  

  * AI-generated images and media  
  

  * Large document creation  
  

  * External data retrieval
  *   
Multi-channel campaign preparation


  
In general:  
  


  * **Simple drafting tasks** typically have the lowest usage cost.  
  

  * **Content generation with structured artifacts** requires additional processing.  
  

  * **Multi-step workflows with approvals and platform actions** generally consume more AI resources.  
  

  * **Image generation and other rendered media** usually increase total session cost.

## **Frequently Asked Questions**  
  


**Q: How is Ask AI pricing calculated?**

Pricing is based on the AI resources consumed during a session, including reasoning, content generation, artifact rendering, external data retrieval, and supported platform actions.  
  


**Q: Does every prompt cost the same?**

No. Costs vary depending on the complexity of the request and the amount of processing required.  
  


**Q: Do approval requests increase session cost?**

Approval workflows involve additional reasoning and structured rendering, which may contribute to overall session usage.  
  


**Q; Does image generation cost more than text generation?**

Generally, yes. Rendering images or other visual assets typically requires more AI resources than generating text alone.  
  


**Q: Can Ask AI complete actions automatically?**

Some supported actions can be completed directly after approval. Others may require connected integrations, platform permissions, or manual setup.  
  


**Q: Why couldn’t Ask AI complete every task in Example 3?**

Some publishing actions required connected social media accounts or an active WhatsApp subscription. Ask AI completed all supported work and identified the remaining prerequisites.
