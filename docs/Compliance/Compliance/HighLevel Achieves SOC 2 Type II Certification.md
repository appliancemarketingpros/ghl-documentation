# **What is SOC 2 Type II?**

  


SOC 2 (System and Organization Controls 2) is a voluntary compliance standard created by the American Institute of CPAs (AICPA). It outlines how organizations should manage customer data based on five specific criteria.

  


We were accredited on Security, Confidentiality, and Availability.

  


A Type I report looks at a company’s security controls at a single point in time. A Type II report is much stricter. It signals that we are the "real deal." We are an enterprise-grade platform that has proven its capabilities. It confirms that our security controls are designed correctly and have operated effectively over time.

## **Why this matters for agencies**

  


Agencies manage sensitive data for clients. Whether you serve local businesses or large enterprises, your clients need to know the platform powering their operations is secure.

  


This accreditation is a tool for you, not just a win for us

  


  1. **Compete with the "Big Guys":** You can now show best-in-class security credentials for the platform running your business. This helps you compete with larger entities.  
  

  2. **Unlock Enterprise opportunities:** Most enterprise-level clients won't even entertain a conversation without a SOC 2 report. It is often a mandatory requirement in their procurement process. This certification removes that barrier, allowing you to close bigger deals.  
  

  3. **A stronger moat:** The process of achieving SOC 2 compliance forces a company to identify and fix vulnerabilities they didn't know they had. By doing this work, we have strengthened our "moat" against data breaches and cyberattacks, giving you a harder, more resilient shell for your business.  
  

  4. **Easier compliance:** When your platform is certified, compliance conversations with prospects become significantly easier.
