# **What is Sending Gift Cards?**

  


Sending a gift card creates a **one-time, prepaid credit** and delivers it to a chosen recipient via email, SMS, PDF, or QR code, **without** requiring the contact to purchase it. This is ideal for make-goods, loyalty gestures, VIP perks, and internal tests. Redemption happens on supported HighLevel-powered checkouts where gift cards are accepted. The Send flow works exclusively in **Live Mode** currently, ensuring only real, fully functional gift cards are delivered.

* * *

## **Key Benefits of Sending Gift Cards**

  


  * **One-to-one issuing:** send a gift card to a single recipient instantly or on a schedule.|  
  


  * **No checkout required:** issue prepaid value without charging the recipient.  
  


  * **Flexible delivery:** deliver via Email, SMS, PDF, or QR depending on your contact data and use case.  
  


  * **Frictionless redemption:** recipients can redeem on supported checkouts with their code or link.  
  


  * **Operational visibility:** track orders, code, amount, and status under Gift Card Orders.  
  


  * **Great for service recovery:** compensate for support issues quickly and professionally.  


* * *

## **Use Cases for Sending Gift Cards**

  


Sending Gift Cards manually is ideal for:

  


  * **Loyalty & Rewards: **Reward customers for milestones, referrals, or premium plan renewals.  
  

  * **Customer Support Compensation:** Issue a gift card instead of a manual credit or refund.  
  

  * **Promotions:** Run giveaways, holiday gifting, influencer campaigns, or targeted perks.  
  

  * **Internal Testing:** QA teams, admins, or developers can test redemption flows.  
  

  * **Bulk Manual Distribution:** Gift VIP customers or event attendees a prepaid balance.


* * *

## **Prerequisites for Sending Gift Cards**

  


Before sending a gift card, ensure:  
  


  1. A **Gift Card Product** exists  
  


  2. The Gift Card Product is **Active** (not Deactivated or Archived)  
  


  3. The customer receiving the card has either:  
  


     * An **email address** , or  
  


     * A **phone number** (for SMS delivery)

## **How to Send a Gift Card**

  


###  _**Step 1:** Navigate to Gift Card_

  


  1. Login to your sub-account.  
  

  2. Go to **Payments → Gift Cards.**  
  
**![](https://jumpshare.com/share/WO8nSGZWS8RLjB2nvM4m+/Screen+Shot+2025-11-26+at+9.02.39+PM.png)**  
  

  3. **Create a Gift Card** or **select an existing Gift Card** from the **All Gift Cards** list.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/lwAqTaYBJlHZUyLQOnHW+/Screen+Shot+2025-11-26+at+9.07.49+PM.png)  
  

  4. On the Gift Card you want to send, click the **Send** button**.** You will now enter the Send flow.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/BfuXE2nejTPExaY10OSk+/Screen+Shot+2025-11-26+at+9.10.09+PM.png)  
  


### _**Step 2:** Choose a Customer/Contact_

  


  1. **Choose the recipient:** Select an existing customer/contact from your list.  
  

  2. or **Add a Contact** before sending.  
  

  3. Click **continue**.   
  
**![](https://jumpshare.com/share/loy8HURAsCLH25bOIfTb+/Screen+Shot+2025-11-26+at+9.15.10+PM.png)**  
  


### _**Step 3:** Add a Personal Message_

  


  * A custom greeting, special instructions, or a note of appreciation.  
  

  * or a message from the pre-populated messages.  
  

  * or a message generated using **Write with AI.**  
  

  * Click **continue.**  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/wUpCkc5IeaOSTyVzG2rN+/Screen+Shot+2025-11-26+at+9.21.05+PM.png)  
  


### _**Step 4:** Select a Denomination_

  


  1. Choose one of the **preset denominations** configured for the product.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/0Jfwr6zimU6IGHcdZIMo+/Screenshot+2025-11-26+at+9.29.28%E2%80%AFPM.png)  
  


### _**Step 5:** Choose Delivery Method_

  

    
    
    **Note:** Only methods supported by the **customer's available information** (email/phone) will be enabled.

  
You can deliver the gift card through any _one_ available methods:  
  


  * **Email**  
  


  * **SMS**  
  


  * **QR Code (via email)**  
  


  * **PDF (via email)**  
  


![](https://jumpshare.com/share/2s6Cy4C4QE85akAC0ifg+/Screen+Shot+2025-11-26+at+9.39.03+PM.png)

  


  


### _**Step 6:** Choose Delivery Date_

  

    
    
    **Note:** A gift card's **available delivery date range** is determined by the **card’s expiry settings**. If the gift card **has an** **expiry date** , the delivery date must be **between today’s date and the expiry date**. If the gift card **does not have an expiry date** , it can be **scheduled for any future date**.

  
You can either:  
  


  * **Presets:** Deliver**immediately, tomorrow** or **after one week.**  
  


  * or schedule**d** elivery**** for a **Future Date.**  
  


  * Click**continue.**  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/fXBLYnUAf7ab0c2nbQEB+/GIF+Recording+2025-11-26+at+9.54.26+PM.gif)  
  


### _**Step 7:** Review & Send_

  


  1. Before confirming, **review** the **automatically generated email or SMS preview**. This includes:  
  

     * Gift Card Amount  
  

     * Custom Message  
  

     * Expiry Date  
  

     * Redemption Instructions  
  

     * Artwork  
  

  2. Click **Send Gift Card.**  
  
**![](https://jumpshare.com/share/Dx07l8CphQzWnfDpO63v+/GIF+Recording+2025-11-26+at+10.20.40+PM.gif)**

## **Key Behaviors & Limitations**

  


  * Send flow works **only in Live Mode.**  
  


  * You can send **1 gift card to 1 customer at a time.**  
  


  * No payment is collected in the Send flow.  
  


  * Directly sent gift cards **do not appear** in Payments → Orders (no order, no transaction).  
  


  * Gift Cards issued via Send are fully redeemable everywhere - both fully and partially.  
  


  * You must have the customer's email or phone number for delivery.

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

  


**Q: When will a scheduled gift card be delivered?**

It will be delivered at **12:00 AM (midnight)** on the **selected** **delivery** **date**.

  


**Q: Can I send gift cards in Test Mode?**

Not yet. Send flow supports Live Mode only. Test Mode support is coming in future releases.

  


**Q: Why can’t I send a gift card to some customers?**

The customer must have:

  * A valid email, or
  * A valid phone number


Otherwise, no delivery method is available.

  


**Q: Can I send multiple gift cards at once?**

Currently no. You can send one gift card to one customer per Send flow. Bulk sending is planned for future versions.

  


**Q: Are sent gift cards redeemable everywhere?**

Yes, on every checkout that supports Gift Card redemption, including:

  * Funnels
  * Forms
  * Invoices
  * Stores
  * Payment Links
  * Calendars (where products are supported)


  


**Q: What happens to a scheduled gift card before the delivery time?**  
Until the scheduled delivery time, the gift card remains in a **Scheduled** state but is still active and valid for use.

  


**Q: Can a scheduled gift card be used before its delivery time?**  
Yes. If someone has the gift card code and attempts to use it at checkout before the scheduled delivery time, it will work.

  


**Q: Can I edit the email/SMS template before sending?**

Templates are system-generated and currently not editable. Email & SMS template customization will be available later.

  


**Q: Do sent gift cards appear under Orders in Payments?**

No, only purchased gift cards appear under Payments → Orders. Sent cards appear exclusively under Gift Card Orders.

  


**Q: Can I send a gift card anonymously?**

Not yet. The recipient always sees the sender’s business information as configured in email/SMS templates.
