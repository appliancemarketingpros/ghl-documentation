# **What are Text-To-Pay Links?**

  


Text-To-Pay Links are secure payment requests you send by SMS from within a conversation. They generate a checkout link so customers can pay in a few taps. This reduces friction, speeds up collection, and keeps payment activity tied to the contact thread for easy follow-up and tracking.

* * *

## **Key Benefits of Text-To-Pay Links**

  


  * **Fast Collection:** Send a pay-ready link from any SMS thread for fewer steps and quicker conversion.  
  


  * **On-Brand:** Customize the invoices to match your brand style.  
  


  * **Flexible Pricing:** Apply discounts and tax at send time to reflect the exact deal.  
  


  * **Due Dates:** Set a link due date to encourage timely payment and manage expectations.


* * *

## **Prerequisites**

  


Verifying prerequisites prevents common setup issues and ensures links send and process correctly.

  


  * An active **HighLevel Payments (Stripe)** connection in the sub-account.  
  


  * An active**phone number** in the sub-account.  
  


  * At least one active product.

## **Sending a Text-To-Pay Link from Conversations**

  


  1. Go to **Conversations** and open the conversation with the contact you would like to send the text-to-pay link to.  
  
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155059937500/original/Z06JS7DfNEdQHNj3HkaoZkBJ4ycTg6iXhg.png?1764888541)  
  

  2. In the channel picker at the message box, choose **SMS**.  
  
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155059937514/original/bkqzsFiLaazDrhnC4ZwP2PVZOAckyAX6Qg.png?1764888582)  
  

  3. Click **Request Payment** ($ icon) below the text editor.  
  
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155059937521/original/gmxcwUzu_sQgY1-l_3evlJWNqMs2SpCYkA.png?1764888617)  
  

  4. In the **Request payment** modal; Select the **product** (or item) and confirm the **price** , add a **discount** and/or**tax** , set a **Due date** and choose between **Test** and **Live** mode (use Test for non-production checks).  
  
Click **Preview** to review the checkout experience, or click **Copy link & Mark as sent**.  
  
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155059937574/original/QPsNg403TjgyWqIV6H04JlwZFcEvXuf1jw.png?1764888764)  
  


  5. Click **Send** to send the text-to-pay link. The customer will see the following screen when opening the link!
