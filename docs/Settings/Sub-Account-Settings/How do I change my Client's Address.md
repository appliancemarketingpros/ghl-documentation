# How do I change my Client's Address?

**Source URL:** [https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/48001204293-how-do-i-change-my-client-s-address-](https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/48001204293-how-do-i-change-my-client-s-address-)  
**Category:** Settings  
**Folder:** Sub-Account Settings

---

You can change your client's address by going to Location View -> Settings -> Business Info -> Company Address tab
