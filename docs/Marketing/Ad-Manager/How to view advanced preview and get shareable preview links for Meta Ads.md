This guide explains how to use **Advanced Preview** and **Shareable Preview Links** in Ad Manager for Meta campaigns. These features allow you to review how your ad appears across placements and share a preview of the published ad with teammates or stakeholders.

## Using Advanced Preview

Advanced Preview allows you to see how your ad creative will render across different Meta placements. This helps ensure that the ad appears correctly before sharing it with others or reviewing campaign performance.  


### Steps to access Advanced Preview

Navigate to **Marketing > Ad Manager** in your sub - account.  
Open the **Meta campaign** you want to review.  
Go to the **Ad Set** and select the **Ad** you want to preview.  
Locate the **Preview panel** on the right side of the campaign builder.  
Click the **Advanced Preview icon** in the preview panel.

The preview opens in a new tab and loads Meta’s preview interface. This interface displays how your ad appears across the placements configured in the campaign.

Scroll through the placements within the preview to verify how your ad creative appears across the Meta ecosystem.

## Placement rendering in Preview

The Advanced Preview attempts to display the ad in all placements configured for the campaign. However, some placements may not support certain creative formats.  
If a placement does not support the uploaded creative format, the preview will display the **error message returned by Meta** explaining why the ad cannot render in that placement. This helps identify compatibility issues without needing to navigate to Meta Ads Manager.  


* * *

## Generating a shareable preview link

Ad Manager allows you to generate a **shareable preview link** that opens the ad inside Meta’s preview environment. This link can be shared with team members or stakeholders to view the ad rendering.

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155066972699/original/w4PJnPIEgpVK9eOmXAlmU7QqsywW-Cq5Ow.png?1773609763)

  


### Availability of the Shareable Link

  


  * The shareable preview link is available **only for published campaigns**.


The link option will **not appear for ads that are in Draft or Paused status**.  


* * *

## Steps to copy the shareable preview link

  1. Open the **published Meta campaign** inside **Ad Manager**.

  2. Navigate to the **Ad level**.

  3. Locate the **Preview panel**.

  4. Click the **link icon** located next to the **Advanced Preview** button.

  5. The preview link will be **automatically copied to your clipboard**.


  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155066972718/original/pk0lEOXFqUZ8j6XqhO2dhvYu_2qDbOSP0w.png?1773609864)

  


You can then paste the link wherever needed to share the ad preview.

* * *

## Viewing the shared preview

Opening the shareable preview link loads the **Meta-hosted ad preview interface,** preferably in a new browser tab. The preview displays the ad creative and allows viewers to see how the ad appears across the configured placements.

This preview represents how the ad would appear to users on Meta surfaces.

## Access permissions

  * Access to the preview follows **Meta’s ad account permission model**. Only users with appropriate access to the associated ad account will be able to view the preview experience.
  * This ensures that the ad preview remains accessible only to authorised users while still allowing teams to review ads easily through the shareable link.
