### **What is an Email Sequence?**

An **Email Sequence** is a set of automated emails sent in order, forming a **single threaded conversation** with your contact.

Each sequence can include:

  * Multiple email steps
  * Time delays between emails
  * Conditions (e.g., send only if email is opened)
  * Automatic stopping when a contact replies


This allows you to create personalised, behavior-based communication without manual follow-ups.

  


### **When Should You Use Email Sequences?**

Email Sequences are ideal for:

  * **Follow-ups:** Automatically follow up with leads who haven’t responded
  * **Welcome Series:** Onboard new users or customers
  * **Reminders:** Send scheduled reminders for events, demos, or actions
  * **Nurture Campaigns:** Gradually engage and educate your audience


Use sequences when you want to maintain **consistent, timely communication** without manual effort.

  


  


### **How to Create an Email Sequence?**

1\. Navigate to Sequences

  * Go to **Marketing → Email → Campaigns**


2\. Create a New Sequence

  * Click on **Create New Sequence![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155073498872/original/Jn2S8no_mGdTsVnWg4LJi17VOQgwtmQWAQ.png?1781199841)**


3\. Set the Trigger

  * Choose when the sequence should start:
    * Immediately
    * At a scheduled time
    * Depending on sequence type configure the "**Stop on Reply** "  
  
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155073498892/original/26ZrWYBVSGpCcXu-XQneAAqnOAyGNnjgTA.png?1781199866)


  
4\. Select Your Audience

  * Choose from:
    * Lists
    * Segments
    * Individual contacts
  * Enable **Stop on Reply** if needed  
  


5\. Add Email Steps

  * Add multiple steps to your sequence
  * Define **delays** between each step![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155073498929/original/ZOSfszurMpzteYezypUOHlsITjbwRewx_g.png?1781199937)

### **How to Pause an Email Sequence?**

  
Users can move a published sequence back to draft. However, any contacts who become eligible for the next step while the sequence is paused or in draft will be skipped.

For example, if Email 2 is set to send only after Email 1 is opened, and Contact A opens Email 1 while the sequence is paused, Contact A will not receive Email 2 and will be skipped.

Also note: once Step 1 has been executed, or the first email in Step 1 has been sent, users cannot pause the sequence to edit the trigger or Email Step 1.

**_FAQs_**

1\. What happens if a contact replies?

If **Stop on Reply** is enabled, the sequence will automatically stop for that contact.

  


2\. Can I send both designed and plain text emails?

Yes, sequences support both **designed templates** and **plain text emails**.

  


3\. Can I control who receives each email?

Yes, you can apply **conditions** to each step (e.g., based on open or delivery status).

  


4\. How do I track performance?

You can view performance metrics at:

  * **Sequence level**
  * **Individual step level**


Access stats from the **three-dot menu → Statistics**.

  


5\. Will emails appear as separate messages?

No, emails are sent as part of a **single threaded conversation** , helping maintain context.
