# **What is Watermark in Social Planner?**

  


A watermark is a visible, often translucent, design or text superimposed onto a digital image or document. HighLevel lets users upload one watermark per connected social account and control which watermark is applied automatically at publish time. Instead of a single global watermark, assignments determine the branding used for each account, helping teams manage multiple brands without repetitive re-uploads.

* * *

## **Key Benefits of Using Watermarks**

  


  * **Ownership and Copyright Protection:** Watermarks can be used to assert ownership and protect the copyright of the content. By including a watermark with a username, logo, or other identifying information, it becomes more difficult for others to claim the content as their own.  
  

  * **Brand Recognition:** For businesses and content creators, watermarks can serve as a form of branding. Placing a logo or a brand name on images helps in building brand recognition. This can be particularly important for marketing and establishing a consistent online presence.  
  

  * **Deterrence Against Unauthorised Use:** Watermarks act as a deterrent against unauthorised use of images. Potential infringers may be less likely to use or share content that is clearly marked with someone else's branding or identification.  
  

  * **Professionalism:** Watermarks can add a level of professionalism to the content. This is especially important for photographers, artists, and other creatives who want to showcase their work in a polished manner.  
  

  * **Promotion and Marketing:** Watermarks can be used strategically for promotional purposes. For example, a watermark might include a website URL, social media handle, or a hashtag related to a campaign, directing viewers to the creator's online presence or a specific promotion.  
  

  * **Personalisation and Artistic Expression:** Some individuals may use watermarks as a means of personalization and artistic expression. The design and placement of can be a creative element that enhances the overall visual appeal of the content.

## **How to Upload & Manage Watermarks**

  


### ** _Step 1:_**_Go to Settings_

  


  1. Login to your sub-account.  
  

  2. Navigate to **Marketing** → **Social****Planner** → **Settings**.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/OXOEQp9Zi7KKKcBuQ1Lu+/Screen+Shot+2025-12-17+at+5.55.28+PM.png)  
  

  3. Click on the **Watermark** tab.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/CtmT0rXnI0P2f1Kc4ivT+/Screen+Shot+2025-12-17+at+6.57.13+PM.png)  
  

  4. If you haven’t added a watermark yet, you’ll see an “**Try It Now** ” nudge prompting you to create one. And if you already had watermark assigned than your existing single watermark will automatically appear in the list view as “**All Socials** ”. You can choose to edit this setup and assign specific watermarks to each account later.  
  
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155060830598/original/LPfzGUC1XNMmgARG3I8RpjwqDspCPalhOA.jpeg?1765977925)  
  


### _**Step 2:** Upload a Watermark_

  


  1. Click on the**\+ Add Watermark** button.  
  

  2. Give your watermark a **name** for easy identification.  
  

  3. Upload a **PNG** or **JPG** (**min**. **200x200px** , **max**. **5MB**).  
  
**![](https://jumpshare.com/share/MjZ0C4Jgmp9vnYcfqnOg+/GIF+Recording+2025-12-17+at+7.01.17+PM.gif)**  
  


### _**Step 3:** Edit Watermark_

  


  1. Adjust the the **alignment** of watermark.  
  

  2. Adjust the **Opacity** of the watermark.  
  

  3. Use the **Scale** button to **resize** the watermark.  
  

  4. Use the Auto Padding option to add more space around the logo.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/eWCkoKR8rXfJpP0ffsYf+/GIF+Recording+2025-12-17+at+7.20.05+PM.gif)  
  


### _**Step 4:** Select Accounts and Create Watermark_

  


  1. Select **All** or **Particular** social media accounts you want to **apply** **this** **watermark** **to**.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/Jj1GrOti56poM1wtPhiy+/Screen+Shot+2025-12-17+at+7.23.49+PM.png)  
  

  2. Click on the **Create** button.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/kB14mH9uo5ZxpjMmUvjc+/Screen+Shot+2025-12-17+at+7.26.48+PM.png)  
  


### **_Step 5:_**_Manage Uploaded Watermarks_

  


Once uploaded, you can:

  


  * **View** all watermarks in a list.  
  


  * **Edit** (rename or replace) any watermark.

## **Important Notes**

  


  * New posts created after the release will use the latest assigned watermark.  
  

  * When you edit or clone an older post, you will see the original (non-watermarked) image, and the new watermark will apply only after saving.  
  

  * New Category Queue posts will have new assigned watermark.  
  

  * RSS and Review Posts does not support watermark.  
  

  * Video watermarking is not supported (API restriction)  
  

  * For automated posts (Category Queue & Recurring Post) the new watermark will start applying from the next scheduled post onwards.

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

  


**Q. Can I apply more than one watermark to the same social account?**

No. Each social account can have **one assigned watermark** at a time.

  


**Q. What happens if I delete a watermark that’s assigned?**

Affected social accounts reset to **No watermark** until you assign another.

  


**Q. Do watermarks apply to videos?**

No. Watermarks apply to **supported image posts** only.

  


**Q. Will changing assignments update posts already scheduled?**

Changes affect **future publishes**. Existing scheduled items keep their media unless you recreate or update them.

  


**Q. Can RSS or Review posts use watermarks?**

No. These sources do **not** apply watermarks.

  


**Q. Is there a recommended file type and size?**

PNG or JPG (min. 200x200px, max. 5MB)

  


**Q. How does bulk CSV scheduling decide which watermark to use?**

It uses the **current per‑social assignment** for each account at the time the post publishes.
