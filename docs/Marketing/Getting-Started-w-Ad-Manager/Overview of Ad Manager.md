# **What is Ad Manager?**

  


Ad Manager brings **Facebook, Instagram, Google, and LinkedIn** Ads together into one easy-to-use dashboard.

Instead of switching between different ad platforms, users can create campaigns, track performance, and optimize results — directly within HighLevel. This saves time, reduces complexity, and makes professional advertising accessible for businesses of all sizes.

  


Ad Manager enables both agencies and sub-accounts to run high-performing campaigns and analyze performance without leaving the HighLevel platform.

* * *

## **Supported Platforms and Campaign Types**

  


Ad Manager supports multiple platforms and ad types. The table below outlines what objectives and formats are available on each platform.

  


**Platform**| **Campaign Objectives**| **Ad Formats Supported**  
---|---|---  
**Meta (Facebook & Instagram)**| Lead Generation, Website Traffic, Engagement, Sales| Single Image, Video, Carousel (up to 10), Lead Forms, Conversation Forms  
**Google Ads**|  Search Campaigns, Demand Generation _(coming soon)_|  Search Ads, Image, Video, Carousel (Demand Gen)  
**LinkedIn Ads**|  Lead Generation, Website Visits| Single Image, Video, Carousel, Lead Forms

## **Key Benefits of Ad Manager**

  


Ad Manager provides powerful advantages for both agencies and sub-accounts by simplifying campaign execution, improving transparency, and enabling measurable results.

  


  * **All-in-One Advertising:** Manage campaigns across Facebook, Instagram, Google, and LinkedIn from a single dashboard.


  


  * **Time-Saving Workflows:** Guided setup helps users launch ads quickly and efficiently.


  


  * **Simplified Targeting:** Reach the right audience through goal-based campaign objectives.


  


  * **Clear ROI:** Gain instant visibility into ad performance, spend, and conversion metrics.


  


  * **Outcome-Focused:** Run lead generation, traffic, or conversion campaigns that drive tangible business results.


  


  * **Operational Efficiency for Agencies:** Manage multiple clients’ campaigns seamlessly.


  


  * **Resell Opportunity:** Agencies can monetize Ad Manager access by offering it to sub-accounts as a value-added service.

## **Pricing & Access**

  


Ad Manager is available **free for agencies** , giving them flexibility to **resell access** to sub-accounts and create new revenue opportunities.

  


### **Key Details**

  


  * **Agencies:** Ad Manager access is included at no cost.


  


  * **Sub-Accounts:** Agencies can choose to resell access and set their own pricing.


  


  * **Profit Control:** Whatever price the agency sets becomes its profit — HighLevel’s base cost is always $0.


  


  


Agencies can manage Ad Manager availability and pricing in three places:

  


  


  * **Agency → Reselling Tab** – Define default pricing for all sub-accounts.

## **How to Set Up Ad Manager**

  


Follow these steps to set up Ad Manager and start running campaigns:

  


**Access Ad Manager:**

  


From your HighLevel dashboard, navigate to **Marketing → Ad Manager**.

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155056046202/original/eEM9a6YJnB7QmmkWTKKstaj2q3xRvVPjWQ.png?1760528861)

  


  


### **Connect Ad Accounts:**

  


Click **Connect Accounts** and follow the prompts to link your Facebook, Instagram, Google, or LinkedIn Ads account.

  


  


### **Set Permissions**

  


Ensure correct permissions are granted for creating and managing campaigns.

  


  


### **Create a Campaign**

  


Choose the platform (Facebook, Instagram, Google, or LinkedIn). Define your campaign objective (e.g., Awareness, Traffic, Conversions, or Lead Generation). Set audience targeting, budget, and schedule.

  


  


### **Monitor Results**

  


Track performance metrics such as impressions, clicks, conversions, and spend in the Ad Manager dashboard.

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

  


**Q: Do agencies pay for Ad Manager?**

No. Agencies get free access to Ad Manager. They may choose to resell it to sub-accounts.

  


**Q: What platforms are supported in Ad Manager?**

Currently supported platforms are **Facebook, Instagram, Google, and LinkedIn Ads**.

  


**Q: Can agencies set their own pricing for sub-accounts?**

Yes. Agencies can define pricing in the Reselling tab, Sub-Account Reselling tab, or SaaS Configurator.

  


**Q: Where can sub-accounts see their reports?**

All performance reports are available within the Ad Manager dashboard in real-time.

  


**Q: Do sub-accounts provide their own ad budgets?**

Yes. Each sub-account is responsible for its own ad spend budget.
