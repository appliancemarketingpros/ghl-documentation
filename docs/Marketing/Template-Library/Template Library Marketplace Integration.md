**Source URL:** [https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/155000007747-template-library-marketplace-integration](https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/155000007747-template-library-marketplace-integration)  
**Category:** Marketing  
**Folder:** Template Library

---

The Template Library is now integrated with the Marketplace, allowing you to discover, install, and sell templates, all in one place.

You can now browse, preview, purchase, and use templates or list your own to monetise, without leaving the platform.

* * *

## _**Overview**_

The Template Marketplace Integration brings together **template discovery and monetisation** into a single unified experience.

Inside the Template Library, you can now:

  * Access Marketplace templates
  * Preview and install templates instantly
  * Filter templates based on pricing and install status
  * Share and sell your own templates via the Marketplace


This eliminates the need to switch between different platforms, making template usage and distribution seamless.

## **_How to Use_**

  


### **To Discover Templates**

  1. Go to **Template Library in agency view**
  2. Click on the **Marketplace** tab
  3. Browse or search for templates
  4. Use filters to refine results


* * *

### **To Install Templates**

  1. Open a template from Marketplace
  2. Click **Preview** to view details
  3. Click **Install**
  4. Complete payment (if applicable)
  5. Access the template under **My Templates**


* * *

### **To Sell Templates**

  1. Open a template in **Template Library**
  2. Click **Preview**
  3. Click **3 dots → Sell on Marketplace**
  4. You’ll be redirected to the **Seller Portal**
  5. Complete listing details and submit


* * *

### **T****o Update a Template (For Sellers)**

  1. Go to your listed template
  2. Click **Refresh Template**
  3. Add details in **What’s New**
  4. Confirm to publish updates to buyers

## ** _Frequently Asked Questions_**

  


**Q: Where can I find Marketplace templates?**  
You can access them via **Template Library → Marketplace tab** or from the **App Marketplace → Templates section**.

  


**Q: Is the payment for templates one-time or recurring?**  
Templates are available on a one-time payment basis. Once purchased, you can use the template without any recurring charges.

  


**Q: What happens after I install a template?  
** The template will be available under My Templates and can be edited and used in builders.

  


**Q: Can I update a template after listing it?**  
Yes, you can refresh your template and share updates with users via the What’s New section.
