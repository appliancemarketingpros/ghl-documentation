### **What is an Email Sequence?**

An **Email Sequence** is a set of automated emails sent in order, forming a **single threaded conversation** with your contact.

Each sequence can include:

  * Multiple email steps
  * Time delays between emails
  * Conditions (e.g., send only if email is opened)
  * Automatic stopping when a contact replies


This allows you to create personalised, behavior-based communication without manual follow-ups.

  


### **When Should You Use Email Sequences?**

Email Sequences are ideal for:

  * **Follow-ups:** Automatically follow up with leads who haven’t responded
  * **Welcome Series:** Onboard new users or customers
  * **Reminders:** Send scheduled reminders for events, demos, or actions
  * **Nurture Campaigns:** Gradually engage and educate your audience


Use sequences when you want to maintain **consistent, timely communication** without manual effort.

**_FAQs_**

1\. What happens if a contact replies?

If **Stop on Reply** is enabled, the sequence will automatically stop for that contact.

  


2\. Can I send both designed and plain text emails?

Yes, sequences support both **designed templates** and **plain text emails**.

  


3\. Can I control who receives each email?

Yes, you can apply **conditions** to each step (e.g., based on open or delivery status).

  


4\. How do I track performance?

You can view performance metrics at:

  * **Sequence level**
  * **Individual step level**


Access stats from the **three-dot menu → Statistics**.

  


5\. Will emails appear as separate messages?

No, emails are sent as part of a **single threaded conversation** , helping maintain context.
