# How to Add Insight Widgets to Your Dashboard from Key Pages

**Source URL:** [https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/155000005275-how-to-add-insight-widgets-to-your-dashboard-from-key-pages](https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/155000005275-how-to-add-insight-widgets-to-your-dashboard-from-key-pages)  
**Category:** Dashboards  
**Folder:** Dashboard Widgets

---

You can now add relevant insight widgets to your dashboards directly from key pages — making it easier to bring analytics into your workflow without switching context.  
  

    
    
    **✨ What’s This For?**  
     Quickly view and track key metrics by bringing **dashboard** **analytics** into your day-to-day workflow.

  


**TABLE OF CONTENTS**

  * Supported Pages
  * Step 1: Navigate to a supported page (see list above).
  * Step 2: Click the Dashboard Insights icon
  * Step 3: Select the dashboard where you want to add widgets.
  * Step 4: Review the suggested widgets and confirm your selection.
  * Step 5: Click Add — the widgets will be added to the chosen dashboard.
  * Step 6: You can stay on the same page or go to the dashboard to view insights.
  * More Pages Coming Soon!
