# **What is the Local Marketing Audit?**

  


The **Local Marketing Audit** is a powerful diagnostic tool designed to help you understand the overall health and performance of your business. It provides a comprehensive analysis across several key areas like listings, reviews, website performance and SEO, helping you identify growth opportunities.

* * *

## **Key Benefits of the Local Marketing Audit**

  


  * **Measure Business Health at a Glance:** Get an in-depth snapshot of how your business is performing across critical marketing and operational dimensions.  
  


  * **Reinforce Agency Value:** Show your clients exactly where their business stands and how your agency is driving improvements.  
  


  * **Boost Visibility into Key Metrics:** Access detailed insights that can guide strategy, optimize performance, and enhance client satisfaction.


* * *

## **How to Generate Local Marketing Audit Reports**

  


#### ** _Step 1:_**_Open Local Marketing Audit_

  


  1. In the left navigation bar, click **Reporting**.  
  

  2. At the top, select the **Local Marketing Audit** tab.  
  

  3. Click the blue **Generate New Report** button.


  


**![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155063703498/original/TLg-rQJ48D7eDLKY91k_ZsXhVGdq91J4-A.png?1769616148)**  


  


#### **_Step 2:_**_Review & Select Business Details_

  


Your Business Name, Address, Phone, Website is automatically pulled from your location information into the Generate Report module. Review and make any needed edits to the information. 

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155063704498/original/_4_6AEx2PSM1uPeb2kEj1_rse-xsMeG0Kw.png?1769616757)  
  


####  _**Step 3:** Review Google Business Profile_

  


If a **Google Business Profile** (GBP) is already connected, you can select it from the dropdown. If not, simply enable the toggle to search Google Maps directly using your business name, address or Google Maps URL.

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155063704727/original/dQ38KCIkLaIpppUAAZFt669huzGtVzvEhg.png?1769616811)

  


  


#### _**Step 4:** Generate Report_

  


Once all information has been added and verified, click **Generate Report**. 

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155063704947/original/lf4aHfo9X6slurRrbU0dCL_nqDvoZBBugg.png?1769616981)

## **Refresh a****Local Marketing Audit**

  


When you update your website, GBP, listings, or tracking, you can refresh the audit instantly. There is now waiting period and no need to recreate the report.

  


To refresh your audit, just click **Refresh Report** at the top of the Local Marketing Audit page.

## **Understanding Your Report (Your Business Profile & Compare Reports)**

  


The report is organized into two primary areas. Use **Your Business Profile** to assess your current state and **Compare Reports** to benchmark against competitors. 

  


### **Your Business Profile**

  


  1. **Google Business Profile Audit:** Evaluate the completeness and optimization level of your GBP listing.  
  


  2. **Website Performance Audit:** Check for speed, responsiveness, SEO-friendliness, and overall user experience.  
  


  3. **Local SEO Heat Map:** Visualize your local visibility across various search terms and geographies.  
  


  4. **Listings Analysis:** Ensure your business information is consistently and accurately listed across platforms.  
  


  5. **Online Reviews Audit:** Analyze review quantity, quality, and response patterns to gauge your reputation health.  
  


  6. **Business Detail Accuracy Check:** Review foundational business data (name, address, phone, etc.) for correctness and consistency.


  


For additional information, see: [Understanding the Grades in Your Marketing Audit Report](<https://help.gohighlevel.com/en/support/solutions/articles/155000003125>)

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155063627755/original/ddXT0OczUFzZvvY8gA6NzL0A0ZM8f3ITIg.png?1769552475)  
  


### **Competitive Analysis (Compare Reports)**

  


Understand how your business stacks up against the competition with our AI-Powered Competitor Analysis. This feature helps you uncover actionable insights by comparing your business's online presence with others in your market. Benchmark your current audit against competitors, section by section (GBP, Listings, SEO, etc.). 

  


The Competitor Analysis covers two critical areas:  
  


  * **Listings Comparison:** Review the accuracy, completeness, and optimization of your business listings compared to your competitors.  
  

  * **Online Reputation (Reviews) Comparison:** Gain insight into review volume, sentiment, and engagement strategies. Understand how your reputation measures up and where you can improve.


  


For additional information on this report, see [Prospecting: Competitor Analysis Report.](<https://help.gohighlevel.com/en/support/solutions/articles/155000005486>)

  

    
    
    **Note:** The analysis is based on published listings available online for both your business and the selected competitors.

## **Audit Report Settings**

  


Centralized Audit Settings let you change once and apply forward. Configure how future audits behave, settings do not modify historical reports. 

  


To open the **Audit Report Settings** ,**** click the **three dot** menu in the top‑right corner and choose **Settings** from the dropdown.  
  
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155063626449/original/t4Q2MJkYxst6S2A4yOTWu9LpkDryHzXtwQ.jpeg?1769549567)  
  


Audit Report Settings includes three tabs: Business Details, Report Generation, and Report Sections.

  


  * **Business Details:** Update Name, Address, Phone (NAP), Website, and choose the Google Business Profile (GBP) used for audit generation.  
  

  * **Report Generation:** Choose how often reports run (Monthly, Quarterly, or Manual only) and enable or disable email delivery for reports  
  

  * **Report Sections:** Decide which audit sections are included and reorder sections to match your priorities.


  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155063626627/original/zcw7-hHMF0QTEX2uGrPVcyIVdgEBWKT8aA.png?1769550042)

## **Report Timeline**

  


The interactive Report Timeline stores prior audit reports for up to 12 months, so you can quickly review past performance, view improvements, and analyze trends.

  


Click **Report Timeline** at the top of the audit to open the Report Timeline. From here you can:  
  


  * Click a date to instantly open an older report.  
  

  * Hover to preview a summary card with key metrics.  
  

  * Quickly compare scores and sections over time

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

  


**Q: Can I refresh an audit anytime?**

Yes. You can re-run the audit instantly with no waiting period and without recreating the report.

  


**Q: Do changes in Settings affect old reports?**

No. Settings changes apply to future audits only; previously generated reports remain unchanged.

  


**Q: Can I use the Competitive Analysis Report without first generating the Local Marketing Audit?**

No, the Competitor Analysis depends on the Audit Report data. You must generate the audit first.

  


**Q: Can I export the audit in other languages?**

Yes. Choose a language from Export PDF or Share Report. This does not change your account language.
