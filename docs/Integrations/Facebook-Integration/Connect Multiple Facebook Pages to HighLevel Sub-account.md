### **Troubleshooting: Facebook Page Not Showing**

  


If the Facebook page you want to connect does not appear in the list during setup, it is typically due to permission or access issues.

  

    
    
    **Important:** Facebook pages are made available based on the permissions of the Facebook user account you connect. HighLevel does not connect directly to Facebook Business Manager or Ad Accounts.

  


  


**Check the following:**

  * Ensure you have **Admin access** to the Facebook Page (not just Editor or partial access)

  * Confirm you are logged into the **correct Facebook profile** connecting

  * During the connection step, make sure you **grant all requested permissions**

  * If access was recently updated, try **disconnecting and reconnecting** Facebook in Settings → Integrations


  


For a complete step-by-step troubleshooting guide (including LeadConnector permissions, Business Manager settings, and lead access issues), refer to:
