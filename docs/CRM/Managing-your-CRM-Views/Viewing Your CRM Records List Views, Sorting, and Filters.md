# Viewing Your CRM Records: List Views, Sorting, and Filters

**Source URL:** [https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/155000004497-viewing-your-crm-records-list-views-sorting-and-filters](https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/155000004497-viewing-your-crm-records-list-views-sorting-and-filters)  
**Category:** CRM  
**Folder:** Managing your CRM Views

---

CRM List View lets you review records in a clean, tabular layout with quick controls to filter, sort, and customize visible columns. This makes it easier to scan large record sets, spot patterns, and take action without jumping between individual record details.

  


List View is available for **Contacts** (currently in **Labs**), **Opportunities** (via a toggle), **Custom Objects** , **Companies** , and **Tasks**.

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

  


**Q: Which CRM areas support List View?**  
A: List View is available for Contacts (currently in Labs), Opportunities (through a toggle), Custom Objects, Companies, and Tasks.

  


**Q: Is List View the default view for all CRM objects?**  
A: For most CRM objects, List View is the default when you open the object from the left navigation or top bar menu. Opportunities uses a toggle to switch to List View.

  


**Q: Can I sort records in List View?**  
A: Yes—sorting is available on supported fields. Custom Objects also support sorting by custom fields from the Sort menu or by clicking a column header.

  


**Q: Are there any sorting limitations for Custom Objects?**  
A: Yes. Some custom field types do not support sorting in Custom Objects List View. For steps and limitations, reference **List View for Custom Objects**.

  


**Q: Can I control which columns appear in List View?**  
A: Yes. You can choose which fields display as columns, then resize and rearrange them.
