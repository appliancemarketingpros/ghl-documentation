# **What is Video Testimonials in Reputation Management?**

  


Educational description:Video Testimonials is a Reputation feature that allows you to collect customer video reviews using shareable, branded “**Video** **Collectors**.” Contacts can record or upload a video from desktop or mobile (no app required). Collected videos can then be reviewed internally and showcased on your site using the Review Widget.

* * *

## **Key Benefits of Video Testimonials**

  


  * **Authenticity:** Real faces and voices build trust far faster than text-only reviews.  
  

  * **Frictionless capture:** Contacts record or upload from any modern device—no app required.  
  

  * **Brand control:** Customize collector branding (logo, background, text, accent, button colors) to match your visual identity.  
  

  * **Flexible distribution:** Share direct links or send via Email, SMS, or WhatsApp from HighLevel.  
  

  * **Centralized management:** View and download all submissions in the Responses tab.  
  

  * **Easy showcasing:** Add videos to your existing Review Widget, with layout options and per‑collector filtering.  
  

  * **Performance guardrails:** Each video is capped at 2 minutes 30 seconds to keep files lightweight and watchable.

## **How to Share & Manage Video Testimonials**

  


### **1\. Via Link**

  


  1. From **Video** **Testimonials** → **Collectors** , **locate** **your** **collector**.  
  

  2. Click **Copy** **Link** and share by **email, chat, social, or QR**.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/0XyIYWbhUeZvpNnjiIR4+/GIF+Recording+2025-11-27+at+9.41.51+PM.gif)  
  


### **2\. Send a Video Review Request (Email/SMS/WhatsApp)**

  


  1. Click **Send** **Review** **Request** or the **share icon** of the collector.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/T9DURcaVubhhe1bwin4T+/Screen+Shot+2025-11-27+at+9.43.15+PM.png)  
  

  2. Select **Contact**.  
  

  3. Select **Email** , **SMS** , or **WhatsApp**.  
  

  4. Use **Override** **Video** **Link** to choose which collector to include.  
  

  5. Select Default Email/SMS/WhatsApp Template or pre-made custom templates.  
  

  6. Click on **Send Review Request**.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/lWRSNbGGsMwhqaikRmEv+/Screen+Shot+2025-11-27+at+9.51.38+PM.png)

## **How to Manage and Download Responses**

  


  1. Open **Reputation** → **Video** **Testimonials** → **Responses**.  
  

  2. **Click** **any** **submission** to view the video.  
  

  3. Click **Download** to save the original file locally.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/5MaLuBcvjHZ4kGMdJ2n5+/GIF+Recording+2025-11-27+at+9.56.04+PM.gif)

## **How to Display Videos in the Review Widget**

  


  1. Go to **Reputation** > **Widgets**.  
  

  2. **Create** or **Edit** a Review Widget.  
  

  3. Select **Review** **Type** as **Video** **Testimonials**.  
  

  4. Choose **Video** **Testimonial** **Source** as the collector you want to display.  
  

  5. Choose **widget** **type** , **number** **of** **reviews** , **override** **video** **link** and **enable** or **disable** **powered by**.  
  

  6. Click **Save**.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/MajrGgax40XeaxAopVog+/GIF+Recording+2025-11-27+at+9.58.58+PM.gif)

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

  


**Q: Do customers need to install an app to record a video?**

No. They can record or upload directly from desktop or mobile using the collector link.

  


**Q: What’s the maximum video length?**

Up to **2** minutes **30** seconds per submission.

  


**Q: How many questions can I add to a collector?**

Up to three questions in the Questionnaire step.

  


**Q: Can I create more than one collector?**

Yes. You can create unlimited collectors per location, and you can clone existing ones.

  


**Q: Where do I find submitted videos?**

Go to Reputation → Video Testimonials → Responses to view and download them.

  


**Q: Can I show only certain videos on my site?**

Yes. Use the per‑collector filter in the Review Widget to display videos from a specific collector.

  


**Q: Which channels support sending the collector link?**

Email, SMS, and WhatsApp via Send Review Request.
