# **What is the GBP Post Scheduler?**

  


The GBP Post Scheduler is a Social Planner-powered tool inside the GBP Optimization workspace that lets agencies and businesses create, AI-generate, schedule, and bulk-publish Google Posts (Updates, Offers, Events) for one or many locations—all without leaving HighLevel. By automating post cadence and centralizing management, you keep every Google listing fresh, relevant, and engaging.

* * *

## **Key Benefits of GBP Post Scheduler**

HighLevel’s scheduler helps you:

  


  * Save time with true “set-it-and-forget-it” publishing.

  * Maintain brand consistency by posting to multiple GBPs at once.

  * Overcome writer’s block—AI creates post copy and images on demand.

  * Improve local SEO; frequent, high-quality posts signal relevance to Google.

  * Track performance and make real-time edits from one dashboard.


* * *

## **Using AI Assist for Content & Images**

AI Assist analyzes your niche and location, then generates keyword-rich text (up to 1,500 characters) and royalty-free imagery sized perfectly for Google (minimum 250 × 250 px, ≤5 MB). Edit the suggestions or accept them in one click for a polished post every time.

## **Connecting Google Business Profiles in Social Planner**

Linking each GBP is required before scheduling. Connect once and reuse across Reputation, Listings, and the Social Planner, ensuring OAuth tokens stay in sync and eliminating duplicate log-ins.

### **Open Social Planner**

  


Navigate to **Marketing** in the left-hand menu, then select **Social Planner** and make sure the **Planner** tab is active at the top. From there, use the account dropdown to choose the correct sub-account and click **Add New Social** to begin connecting a Google Business Profile.

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155061305725/original/SCHKWnnDFFYuxi_ANBKhR-CpNuOBRpOKQQ.png?1766578315)

  


  


### **Connect GBP**

  


Select **Connect GBP** from the list of available social platforms to begin linking a Google Business Profile. This option starts the Google authorization flow, allowing HighLevel to securely access and manage posts for the selected business location.

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155061305792/original/gVaB6wvC6gKSiu0myukSWJ7IysG36EtHSw.png?1766578387)

  


  


### **Select GBP Location**

  


After clicking **Connect GBP** , you’re prompted to choose the Google account you want to use, and HighLevel automatically fetches all Google Business Profiles associated with that account. Select the correct location from the list and click **Add** to connect it, enabling HighLevel to manage and schedule posts for that business.

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155061305932/original/uk1q_AO6nwZKUoaVk_BAHYZkOmRHhJ4aYQ.png?1766578508)

  


  


## **Creating New GBP Post**

  


GBP supports three formats Updates for general news, Offers for time-bound promotions, and Events for happenings with start/end dates. Selecting the right type ensures Google shows your content in the most impactful placement on the listing.

  


  


### **Create New Post**

  


Click **New Post** in the top-right corner and select **Create New Post** to open the post composer in Social Planner. This is the starting point for creating a Google Business Profile post, where you’ll add content, select profiles, and configure scheduling options.

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155061306149/original/sDZ1Jpz3tFNaLKgXdIB6136JRXGC9UcWxw.png?1766578612)

  


  


### **Select GBP Profile**

  


Use the **Post to** dropdown to view all connected social profiles and select the Google Business Profile location where the post should be published. Once selected, that GBP appears checked in the list, confirming the post will be created and scheduled specifically for that business location.

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155061306450/original/HXzyBdIMmpNpYbIxluOdtxHNeX3zVie9Ag.png?1766578845)

  


  


### **Compose GBP Post**

  


Enter your message in the content editor, keeping the **1,500-character limit** in mind, and use the formatting tools to enhance readability, such as bold text, emojis, hashtags, and links. You can also click **AI** to generate optimized copy, add an image to make the post more engaging, and use the **Google Business Profile options** below to choose the post type (Update, Offer, or Event) and optionally add a call-to-action button.

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155061306803/original/GCNzySWTydYQrZJbkU7E8FZPTd3qNUe5UQ.png?1766579011)

  


  


### **Schedule or Publish**

  


After clicking **Post** , choose how the GBP post should be handled based on your workflow and timing. Select **Schedule Post** to publish on a specific date and time, **Post Now** to publish immediately, or **Send Post for Approval** if the post needs review before going live, then confirm your selection to complete the process.

  


Once the post is scheduled, it is automatically added to the **Scheduled Posts** tab within **Reputation → GBP Optimization**. From there, you can review upcoming posts, make edits, reschedule, duplicate, or delete them before they go live, ensuring full visibility and control over all planned GBP content.

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155061307180/original/_jMXxEFpvIl256BHOTk3Y_xG2jOu_Nhjyw.png?1766579189)

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

  


**Q: How many characters can a GBP post include?**

Up to 1,500 characters per Google post.

  


**Q: Can I schedule video posts to GBP?**

Google currently allows only images (JPEG/PNG ≤5 MB); video is not supported through the API.

  


**Q: Does bulk posting violate Google’s duplication policy?**

Identical content across locations is permitted, but we recommend location-specific tweaks to avoid perceived spam and to maximize local relevance.

  


**Q: What happens if a connected GBP token expires?**

The post will fail. HighLevel flags the profile as “Action Required”; reconnect and reschedule to publish.

  


**Q: Will edits sync to Google instantly?**

Yes. Updating a scheduled post before its publish time overwrites the draft; editing after publication sends an immediate update to Google within a few seconds.

  


**Q: Does AI Assist cost extra?**

AI credits follow your account’s global AI usage. No separate fee is charged for GBP posts.

  


**Q: Are rate limits shared across profiles?**

Yes Google’s limit of 100 posts per 24 hours applies per user token, so large agencies should stagger schedules.
