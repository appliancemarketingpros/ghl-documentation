# **What is Listings Performance Recap Reports?**

  


Listings Performance Recap Reports are branded, automated emails and in-app digests that grade each location’s online listings (score out of 100) and highlight key visibility, engagement, and action items. 

  


By combining profile-completion health with live Analytics data, the report shows clients exactly where they shine and where to focus next without any manual work.

* * *

## **Key Benefits of Listings Performance Recap Reports**

  


  * Hands-free proof of value scheduled summaries keep your agency top-of-mind


  


  * Data-driven retention clients receive concrete visibility and engagement stats


  


  * Action-oriented each report recommends the three quickest wins to boost scores


  


  * Flexible cadence send monthly, quarterly, or half-yearly to match client needs


  


  * Unified branding logo, colors, and sender domain carry your agency’s identity


  


  * At-a-glance dashboard card shows last score + recent improvement for quick check-ins


* * *

## **Automated Reports**

  


Automated Reports generate on the schedule you set and reach clients by email and in-app notifications. Each recap includes:

  


  * Listings Performance Score (0–100)


  


  * Profile Completion %


  


  * Views, clicks, CTR trends pulled from Listings Analytics


  


  * Top 3 recommended actions e.g., “Add appointment URL” or “Sync to Apple Maps”


  


  * White-labeled header and footer that match your agency branding


* * *

## **Listings Score Calculation**

  


The new scoring engine blends three pillars so clients see a single, easy-to-track grade.

  


  * **Visibility:** directory sync status, profile completeness, Google Business Profile link


  


  * **Engagement:** listing views, clicks, calls, and CTR trajectory


  


  * **Actionability:** freshness of data, onboarding tasks completed, prior recap interactions


  


Scores update automatically each time a recap is generated, and the dashboard card compares the latest score to the previous period.

* * *

## **Report Preferences**

  


Report Preferences allow agency and location admins to choose exactly who gets what and when.

  


  * **Frequency selector:** Monthly | Quarterly | Half-Yearly


  


  * **Recipient management:** mix internal users, clients, and custom emails


  


  * **Delivery channels:** Email (with custom “from” domain) and/or in-app bell notifications


  


  * Preview mode to test branding before activating the schedule


  


  * Unified recipient UI also powers other Listings notifications for streamlined control


* * *

## **Dashboard Enhancements**

  


Listings now features a “Performance Recap” card at the top of the Listings dashboard. The card displays:

  


  * Current Listings Performance Score


  


  * Change (+/-) versus last period


  


  * Quick-link to open the full recap report


  


You’ll also notice a Profile Completion Progress Meter beside each location, showing exactly which attributes are still missing for maximum score.

## **How To Setup Listings Performance Recap Reports**

  


Set up once let HighLevel handle the rest.

  


### **Open Listings Analytics**

  


From the left navigation menu, click **Reputation** , then select **Listings** and open the **Analytics** tab. This is where you can view listings performance data and access reporting options for scheduling automated recap reports.

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155062938351/original/nK3hS3tTiiDLSOjEbL_fWyi-z-ykldH1WQ.png?1768813983)

### **Schedule Report**

  


In the **Analytics** view, click **Schedule Report** in the top-right corner to open the reporting options. From the dropdown, choose **Schedule New Report** to begin creating an automated listings performance recap.

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155062938441/original/QM2bjd7rDidCmOMmOj0hK7SfXsaHgJFeIw.png?1768814041)

  


### **Select Entity**

  


In the **Schedule Report** panel, use the **Select Entity for Scheduling** dropdown to choose the business location or entity for which the report will be generated. This ensures the performance recap pulls analytics and listings data for the correct business.

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155062939616/original/uldtFbatE3cEeezbJ6smesl9zF5zYe1gHA.png?1768814611)

  


  


### **Choose Analytics**

  


Under **Choose Analytics Graphs** , select the metrics you want to include in the performance recap report, such as impressions, actions, profile views, and device usage. These selections determine which charts and data points appear in the automated report sent to recipients.

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155062939823/original/TDTkTyXQ3wzArnBU1nyCw2vtQ1ZCq5fFEA.png?1768814689)

  


  


### **Customize Branding**

  


In the **Branding Guidelines** section, confirm or update the agency logo and enter a **Report Headline** and **Report Description**. These details control how the recap report appears to recipients and help ensure it aligns with your agency’s branding and messaging.

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155062940380/original/xBEMR0HLcu8I5drs_GIssuTNifIsJmwIKw.png?1768814962)

  


  


### **Set Frequency**

  


In the **Scheduling Details** section, choose how often the performance recap should be sent—**Monthly** , **Quarterly** , or **Half-Yearly**. The selected frequency controls the reporting period and determines when recipients receive updated listings performance summaries.

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155062940750/original/Ad53cfK8VAh3hYvEklqaYJ_sSkd1f7wDlQ.png?1768815143)

  


  


### **Set Schedule**

  


Use the **At** and **Starting On** fields to define the exact time and start date for the automated report delivery. This determines when the first recap is sent and establishes the timing for all future scheduled reports.

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155062940981/original/hwvZHiwUS9FcSybfKq8e_SjdLPQIQ_O7Jg.png?1768815222)

  


### **Select Language**

  


In the **Language** section, choose the language in which the performance recap report will be generated. This ensures the report content is localized appropriately for the recipients receiving the automated update.

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155062941282/original/SFgIn31WFbf_DmxQeQ93LNQPnHSpkROZ_w.png?1768815352)

  


### **Send Notifications**

  


In the **Send Notifications** section, choose whether the report should be delivered via **In-App** , **Email** , or both. Then select the users or email addresses that should receive the automated listings performance recap.

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155062943673/original/t-awpuGPX2c5L5DU3mAI3UMrbIPSOwKIpg.png?1768816337)

  


### **Configure Email**

  


In the **Email Settings** section, enter the **From Email** , customize the **Subject** , and review or edit the **Email Content** that recipients will see. These settings control how the listings performance recap email is branded and communicated when delivered via email.

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155062943892/original/sAgMnMcp9gXr1NReTiryktVHXMBDGj-X9w.png?1768816460)

  


### **Save Schedule**

  


Review all report settings, then click **Schedule Report** to activate the automated listings performance recap. Once saved, the schedule runs automatically based on the selected frequency, timing, and delivery preferences.

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155062944254/original/NU-fy3Pse6-DrcjajYFhfeyH1CVP18whFg.png?1768816608)

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

**Q: Who can create or edit a Recap Report schedule?**

Agency owners, agency admins, and location admins with Listings permission.

**Q: Can I send the recap only to my team and not the client?**

Yes simply leave client emails off the recipient list. You can add or remove recipients anytime.

  


**Q: How is the Performance Score weighted?**

Visibility, Engagement, and Actionability each contribute roughly one-third, but HighLevel dynamically weights sub-metrics (e.g., GBP sync) based on industry benchmarks.

  


**Q: What if a location’s listings aren’t fully synced yet?**

The Visibility pillar will reflect lower completeness, and the report’s action list will prioritize finishing the sync.

  


**Q: Can I change the report cadence later?**

Absolutely, edit the schedule and pick a new frequency; the next run will follow the updated cadence.

  


**Q: Do recipients need a HighLevel login to view the report?**

No. Email recipients receive a fully branded PDF and summary inline; in-app notification recipients open the report directly inside HighLevel.

  


**Q: Will the recap include analytics from Uberall integrations?**

At launch, scoring uses Yext-powered metrics. Uberall analytics integration is on the roadmap.

  


**Q: How far back does the report pull data?**

Each recap compares the selected period (30 / 90 / 180 days) to the immediately previous period so trends are clear.
