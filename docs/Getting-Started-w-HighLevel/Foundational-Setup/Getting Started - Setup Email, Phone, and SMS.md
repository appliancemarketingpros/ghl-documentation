# **Setup Email**

  


Setting up a dedicated email domain ensures better inbox placement, higher deliverability, and a stronger sender reputation.

  


### **Steps**  
  


  1. Navigate to **Settings → Email Services**.  
  
  


_![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155058341148/original/p1uuASqVD_gUorwClGdjJsDbkvOGbDv4Cg.png?1763065965)_  
  


  2. Click**Dedicated Domain and IP**.  
  


  3. Click **Add Domain**.  
  


_![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155058341567/original/-4I43XNEJPeJp7W6ONjNQwHUrOWzOxkItA.png?1763066983)_  


  4. Enter your subdomain (for example, lc.mydomain.com) and click **Add & Verify**.  
  


  5. Follow the on-screen DNS record instructions from your registrar.  
  


  6. Click **Verify Records** once the updates are complete.  
  


  7. Click the **3-dot menu → Set Headers**.  
  


_![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155058341607/original/EjWmtRa3meoFv9y5e5I1i0Gj2kasFgP_rA.png?1763067103)_  
  


  8. Enter your**From Name** and **From Email** , then click **Save**.


  


Your email setup is now complete and ready for verified sending.

## **Setup Phone**

  


Adding a phone number allows you to make and receive calls directly from within HighLevel.

  


### **Steps**  
  


  1. Go to **Settings → Phone System** and**** Click **Add Number → Add Phone Number**.  
  
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155067473684/original/zXamnRL6zAN3RZJqdA2I4K1RQlfUTxvrQQ.png?1774260664)  
  


  2. Filter numbers by **Country** or **Area Code**.  
  


  3. Choose your preferred number.  
  


  4. Click **Proceed to Buy**.


  


Your number will appear under **Manage Numbers** once added.

## **What is Bulk SMS**

  


Bulk SMS lets you send one SMS message to multiple contacts simultaneously. It’s ideal for marketing campaigns, service notifications, or time-sensitive updates. This feature ensures consistent communication with your audience without needing to message each contact individually.

* * *

## **Key Benefits of Sending Bulk SMS**

  


Using Bulk SMS helps streamline communication while maintaining personalization and delivery control.  
  


  * **Save time:** Message hundreds or thousands of contacts with one action.  
  


  * **Consistent communication:** Ensure all recipients receive the same information.  
  


  * **Personalized content:** Use custom fields and templates for tailored messages.  
  


  * **Flexible delivery options:** Choose to send instantly, schedule for later, or drip messages in batches.  
  


  * **Track results:** Monitor delivery and performance through the Bulk Actions dashboard.

## **How to Send Bulk SMS via Contacts**

  


Follow these steps to create and send an SMS campaign directly from your Contacts tab.

####   
**_Step 1:_**_Select Contacts_

  


Go to **Contacts** and use the checkboxes on the left to select the recipients for your message.

  


_  
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155058342922/original/lDvptq2pRwRDPNvkV6yceTz8XMKCDqYabA.jpeg?1763069378)_

####   
_**Step 2:** Open the Bulk SMS Window _

  
Click the **SMS icon** in the top menu bar to open the Bulk SMS setup window.  
  


_![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155058342921/original/1ZYiKri7cJEzz8qKVYHgcwPDeKhf1lptdA.jpeg?1763069378)_  
**_Step 3:_**_**** Select the Sending Phone Number_

  
From the **From Phone Number** dropdown, choose the number to send from.  
  


If left blank, the system uses the last number used per contact or the account default.

####   
**_Step 4:_**_Compose or Select a Message Template_

  
Type your custom message or select a saved template from **SMS Templates**.  
  


You can personalize messages using custom fields and add emojis or attachments if needed.  
  


_![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155058342923/original/ihr8RdkQ8Dx_ieTlrQQlVtXcRcoi6SIPJg.jpeg?1763069378)_  
**_Step 5:_**_Choose a Delivery Method_

####   
**Send All at Once**

  


Immediately sends your message to all selected contacts.

  


#### **Send at Scheduled Time**

  


Set a specific date and time for delivery.

  


#### **Send in Drip Mode**

  


Sends messages in smaller batches for improved pacing and deliverability.

  


**Drip Mode fields:**  
  


  * **Pick a Start Date & Time:** When sending begins.  
  


  * **Number of Messages per Batch:** Number of contacts per send batch.  
  


  * **Repeat After:** Time delay between each batch (in minutes, hours, or days).  
  


  * **Send On:** Select specific weekdays for sending.  
  


  * **Time Window:** Define allowed sending hours based on account timezone.  
  


  * **End At:** The latest time messages can be sent each day; messages outside this window resume the next day.  
  


_![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155058342920/original/W84znlfIZeBiArRa4ORKsRyyvb0e-ocM_Q.png?1763069378)_

####   
**_Step 6._**_Send the SMS_

  
Click **Send SMS** to submit your campaign. The action will be queued and processed automatically.

  


_![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155058342918/original/dIqeYOGfnqOGWLpEVA2bzdzfJxIjm3NiFg.png?1763069378)_

**_Step 7:_**_Track Results in Bulk Actions_

  
Go to **Contacts → Bulk Actions** to view campaign status and performance metrics such as sent, delivered, failed, or skipped messages.  
  


Click **Show Stats** for detailed delivery reports, including carrier responses and failure reasons.

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

  


**Q: What happens if I don’t select a “From” phone number?**

The system uses the last number used for that contact or defaults to the account’s primary number.

  


**Q: Can I cancel a scheduled bulk SMS?**

Sent messages cannot be canceled. Contact Support immediately for help with scheduled or drip sends.

  


**Q: How does Drip Mode handle time zones?**

Drip Mode uses your account’s local time zone.

  


**Q: Will unsubscribed contacts receive messages?**

No. HighLevel automatically excludes unsubscribed or DND contacts to maintain compliance.

  


**Q: Can I track failed messages?**

Yes. Go to **Bulk Actions → Show Stats** for detailed error reports.

  


**Q: Can I use the same number for calls and SMS?**

Yes, as long as the number is enabled for both.

  


**Q: Why verify my email domain?**

A verified domain ensures better inbox placement and prevents spam issues.

  


**Q: How can I increase messaging limits?**

Contact Support to request an increase after demonstrating consistent, compliant sending activity.
