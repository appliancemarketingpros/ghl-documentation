## **What is Prospect Status Management in ****Prospecting?****  
**

  


Prospect status management is a Prospecting feature that helps users track what happens after a lead is generated. Instead of keeping all prospects in one undifferentiated list, users can now update each prospect’s outcome and filter prospects by lifecycle stage.

  


This feature includes:  
  


  * Prospect Statuses: Open, Closed Won, Closed Lost  
  

  * Status-based filters  
  

  * Create Sub-Account for won prospects in Agency Prospecting  
  

  * A Getting Started onboarding card for new Prospecting users


  


This fills a gap in existing Prospecting coverage, which focuses more on lead generation than post-outreach tracking.

* * *

  


## **Key Benefits of Prospect Status Management in Prospecting  
****  
**

  * Track prospect outcomes directly inside Prospecting  
  

  * Separate active leads from closed leads  
  

  * Identify wins that still need onboarding  
  

  * Reduce manual tracking in spreadsheets  
  

  * Help new users get started faster with guided onboarding  
  

  * Connect prospecting activity to sub-account creation in Agency view

## **How to Prospect Status Management in ****Prospecting**

###   
**Update a Prospect’s Status**  
  


  1. Open Prospecting.  
  

  2. Open the Prospect Card for the record you want to update.  
  

  3. Change the status to Open, Closed Won, or Closed Lost.

**Filter Prospects by Status**  
  


  1. Go to your saved prospects list in Prospecting.  
  

  2. Open the Status filter.  
  

  3. Select one or more of these options:  

     * Open  
  

     * Closed Won  
  

     * Closed Lost

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

  


**Q. What statuses can I assign to a prospect?  
** You can mark a prospect as Open, Closed Won, or Closed Lost.

  
**Q. Can I filter prospects by status?  
** Yes. You can filter by Open, Closed Won, Closed Lost, Sub-Account Created, and Sub-Account Not Created.

What happens when I mark a prospect as Closed Won?  
  
**Q. In Agency Prospecting, marking a prospect as Closed Won makes Create Sub-Account available.****Does this replace categories?  
** No. Categories and status serve different purposes. Categories organize prospects, while status tracks prospect outcome. The categorization article is separate and would need a short cross-reference for this distinction.

  
**Q. Who sees the Getting Started card?  
** It is intended for users who are new to Prospecting. It hides automatically once users become active or dismiss it enough 

times, based on the release details you provided.
