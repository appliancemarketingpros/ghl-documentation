## **What is Prospecting Free Tier in Agency Sub-Accounts?**

  


Prospecting Free Tier in Agency Sub-Accounts allows agencies to access core Prospecting functionality—such as lead generation, audit reports, and CRM syncing—directly inside Sub-Accounts that are marked as agency-owned. This removes the previous limitation where Prospecting was only available in the Agency view unless upgraded to Premium.

This capability is especially valuable for agencies that manage their internal sales and marketing workflows within Sub-Accounts, enabling a more integrated and efficient process from prospect discovery to outreach and follow-up.

* * *

Understanding the advantages of this feature helps agencies fully utilize Prospecting within their existing workflows and systems.

## **Eligibility & Access Requirements**

  


Knowing who can access this feature ensures proper setup and avoids confusion during configuration.

  


  * Available only for **Sub-Accounts marked as “Agency Sub-Account”**
  * Feature must be enabled via **Labs toggle**
  * Requires **$497 Agency Plan** to mark Sub-Accounts as agency-owned  
  

  * Not enabled by default for all Sub-Accounts

## **How To Setup Prospecting Free Tier in Agency Sub-Accounts**

  


Proper setup ensures that Prospecting is correctly enabled and accessible within eligible Sub-Accounts.

**  
**

### **_Step 1:_**_Mark Sub-Account as Agency Sub-Account_

  


  * Go to **Agency View**
  * Navigate to **Sub-Accounts**
  * Locate the desired Sub-Account  
  

  * Click the **3-dot menu (bottom right)**
  * Select **“Add as Agency Sub-Account”**

### ** _Step 2:_**_Access Prospecting in Sub-Account_

  


  * Open the **Sub-Account**
  * Navigate to **Marketing → Prospecting**
  * Click **“Add Prospect”** to begin generating leads or audit reports

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

  


**Q: Why don’t I see Prospecting in my Sub-Account?**  
Ensure the Sub-Account is marked as an Agency Sub-Account and the Labs toggle is enabled.

  


  


**Q: Does this replace Premium Prospecting?  
** No, Premium Prospecting is still required for advanced and client-facing use cases.

  


  


**Q: Can all Sub-Accounts access this feature?  
** No, only Sub-Accounts explicitly marked as Agency Sub-Accounts are eligible.

  


  


**Q: Is there a plan requirement?**  
Yes, marking a Sub-Account as Agency-owned requires the $497 Agency plan.

  


  


**Q: Will leads sync automatically into the CRM?**  
Yes, prospects generated will sync into Contacts and Opportunities within the Sub-Account.

  


  


**Q: Can I run automations on generated leads?  
** Yes, workflows and campaigns can be triggered immediately after lead creation.
