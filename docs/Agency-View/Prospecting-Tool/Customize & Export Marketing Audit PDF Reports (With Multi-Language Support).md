# **What is PDF Customization in Marketing Audit Reports?**

  


PDF Customization allows you to apply your Brand Theme, header, and footer settings from the Prospecting Report Builder directly to exported PDF audits. Every PDF automatically mirrors your web report’s branding, no extra design work required. This creates a seamless prospect experience and saves valuable setup time.

  

    
    
    **Note:** This feature **requires Premium Prospecting subscription**. Preview mode is free of cost for both Agencies & Locations.

* * *

## **Key Benefits of PDF Customization**

  


  * **Consistent branding:** Web links and PDFs always match for a unified, on-brand experience.  
  

  * **Client-ready:** Professional presentation that builds trust with prospects.  
  

  * **Set once, sync everywhere:** Configure in Report Builder and changes apply to all reports.  
  

  * **Brand-matched styling:** Customize border and heading colors to fit any palette.  
  

  * **Clear contact details:** Show agency info on cover and footer so prospects can reach you fast.

## **How to Access the Report Builder in Prospecting Tool**

  


The Report Builder is available at **both the Agency and Sub-Account levels** for users with a **Premium** **Prospecting** **Subscription**. If you're on Basic Prospecting, you can explore the Report Builder, but saving changes is restricted.

  


### **_At the Agency Level_**

  


  1. Log in to your **Agency** account.  
  

  2. Click on the **Prospecting** tab in the main navigation.  
  

  3. Select the **Report Builder** tab to begin customizing your reports.


  


![](https://jumpshare.com/v/8pGNdQIymy3P4VACl5iU+/Screen+Shot+2025-07-07+at+9.42.04+PM.png)

  


  


### **_At the Sub-Account Level_**

  


  1. Login to the **sub-account**.  
  

  2. Click on the **Marketing** tab.  
  

  3. Click on **Prospecting**.  
  

  4. Click the **Report Builder** tab to start building reports.


  


![](https://jumpshare.com/v/q2r18oyQVX3NUdA3iAiB+/Screen+Shot+2025-07-07+at+9.46.07+PM.png)

## **How To Setup and Use PDF Customization**

  


Proper setup ensures every report looks flawless and saves you from manual edits.

  

    
    
    **Note:** Web-only header/footer blocks will not appear in PDFs.

  


### _**Step 1:** Configure Branding & Theme_

  


  1. **Brand Name & Contact Details** – Add your agency name, phone, email, and website in Report Builder.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/DsQNQ3uFjWlSWeHCkniT+/Screenshot+2025-10-02+at+6.05.06%E2%80%AFPM+%281%29.png)  
  

  2. **Borders & Colors** – Customize border colors to align with your brand.  
  

  3. **Heading Colours** – Match section titles (like Local Rank Tracker, Tech Stack Analysis, etc.) with your brand colours.  
  

  4. For a detailed guide on customizing reports, checkout our article on [Prospecting Tool - Fully Customizable Prospect Report Builder](<https://help.gohighlevel.com/en/support/solutions/articles/155000005558>)  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/2WaHW6naTKrLmio6qq5k+/Screenshot+2025-10-02+at+6.04.06%E2%80%AFPM.png)  
  


###  _**Step 2:** Preview & Save Your Report_

  


  1. Click **Preview** to generate a live preview of the report design.  
  

  2. Scan for brand color application on borders, headings, and header/footer details.  
  

  3. Click **Save** to apply selections to the builder and to any **future PDF or web report** without additional steps.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/QuqoMeQj55SuPUp13qG8+/GIF+Recording+2025-10-09+at+9.39.47+PM.gif)  
  


### _**Step 3:** Export a PDF Report_

  


  1. Go to **Prospecting** → **All** **Accounts** (prospect list).  
  

  2. For the prospect you want, either click **View** **Report** and then **Export** **PDF.**  
  
**![](https://jumpshare.com/share/WvjWQ0ghnPtZSrfIMN7a+/GIF+Recording+2025-10-09+at+9.50.06+PM.gif)**  

  3. Or click Export PDF directly from the list and choose the language for the PDF. All system-generated text within the exported PDF — including section headings, button labels, and report messaging — automatically respects the selected language.  
  

  4. Click **Export** **PDF.**  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/0SgZyl51esAFtS7qgnzQ+/GIF+Recording+2025-10-09+at+9.54.03+PM.gif)  
  

  5. Once ready, the PDF report will open in a new tab with all the brand customizations made previously.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/NA3YCMEoKveas0EBBHuS+/GIF+Recording+2025-10-09+at+9.58.42+PM.gif)

## **Frequently Asked Questions**  
  


**Q: Is multilingual PDF export supported?**

Yes. You can select the preferred language when exporting the PDF. All system-generated text inside the report — including headings, prompts, and labels — automatically adapts to the selected language.

  


**Q: Will my agency logo scale correctly in the PDF header?**

Yes, the logo field in Header Customization auto-scales images up to 300 px wide for optimal print quality.

  


**Q: Does this feature require Premium Prospecting?**

Yes.

  


**Q: Can I see my changes in real-time?**

Yes, the builder provides a live preview, ensuring you can see adjustments immediately before publishing.
