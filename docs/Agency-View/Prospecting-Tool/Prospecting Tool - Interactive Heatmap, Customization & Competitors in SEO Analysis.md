# **What is Interactive Heatmap, Customization & Competitors?**

  


This feature transforms local SEO analysis by allowing you to tailor heatmaps for different services and geographic areas. By incorporating custom keywords, adjustable radii, varied grid sizes, a dedicated keyword ranking score, and detailed pin-level competitor insights, it provides a comprehensive and interactive view of your local search landscape.

  

    
    
    **Note:** This feature requires **Premium** **Prospecting** subscription for both Agencies & Locations.
    

* * *

## **Key Benefits of Interactive Heatmap, Customization & Competitors**

  


  * **Custom Keyword Targeting:** Tailor analyses with keywords specific to different service offerings.  
  

  * **Flexible Heatmap Setup:** Adjust coverage radii and grid sizes for both quick scans and in-depth reviews.  
  

  * **Performance Benchmarking:** Use a dedicated keyword ranking score to quickly measure visibility.  
  

  * **Competitor Insights:** Access detailed data with clickable Google Business Profile links.  
  

  * **Focused Reporting:** Share only the most relevant heatmaps to keep reports clear and client-friendly.


* * *

## **Custom Keyword Heatmaps**

  


Custom Keyword Heatmaps enable you to analyze multiple services by simply entering any keyword of your choice. This flexibility allows you to run side-by-side analyses that are finely tuned to reflect the most valuable services for your prospects.

  


  * Type in any keyword (e.g., “drain cleaning” or “roof repair”) and rerun the grid on the fly.  
  

  * Configure different radius sizes (0.5–30 miles) and grid sizes (3×3, 5×5, 7×7) to match diverse business needs.  
  

  * Create multiple heatmaps with distinct configurations for comprehensive comparisons.  
  

  * When generating Web URLs or PDFs of prospect reports, select which heatmaps to include for targeted reporting.


* * *

## **Adjustable Coverage Radius**

  


Adjustable Coverage Radius lets you fine-tune the area that the heatmap represents, ensuring it aligns perfectly with a business’s true service footprint.

  


  * Choose a coverage radius between 0.5 and 30 miles (1–50 km).  
  

  * Easily modify the radius to suit varying service territories and local market dynamics.


* * *

## **Flexible Grid Sizes**

  


Flexible Grid Sizes provide you with options to adjust the depth and granularity of your analysis. Depending on your needs, you can opt for a quick overview or a more detailed investigation.

  


  * Available grid options include 3×3 for rapid assessments, 5×5 for balanced insights, and 7×7 for deep-dive analysis.  
  

  * Select a grid size that best suits the specific area and competitive environment you are evaluating.


* * *

## **Keyword Ranking Score**

  


The Keyword Ranking Score card summarizes the average search ranking across all heatmap pins, allowing you to gauge overall keyword performance at a glance.

  


  * Automatically calculates and displays the average rank for the chosen keyword.  
  

  * Eliminates the need for manual scanning, saving valuable time.


* * *

## **Pin-Level Competitor Insights**

  


Pin-Level Competitor Insights deepen your local analysis by showing the prospect's exact rank at individual locations alongside a list of the top 10 competitors, each with clickable GBP links.

  


  * View the precise rank of the prospect at any pin location.  
  

  * Access an expanded list of up to 10 competitors instead of the previous limit of three.  
  

  * Click on competitor links to quickly review their Google Business Profiles.

## **How To Use Interactive Heatmap, Customization & Competitors **

  


### **For Agency Accounts**

  


####  _**Step 1:** Open Prospecting Tool_

  


  1. Sign in to your **Agency** account.  
  

  2. From the left-hand menu, click on the **Prospecting** tab.  
  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155055207461/original/-W5RdMXe0_kDl4b6mcO7Gz6W3OMGJYQzBQ.jpeg?1759504953)

  
  


#### _**Step 2:** Open a Prospect Report_

  


  1. Click **Add Prospect** to **create** a **new****one** or select an **existing****prospect**.  
  

  2. Click the **View****Report** button.


  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155055215601/original/n62nDmT96zWwsqH8TqzaDcS6LV8gGoMUkg.jpeg?1759510113)

  
  


#### _**Step 3:** Add Custom Keyword_

  


  1. When the report loads, click on the **SEO Analysis** tab in the left sidebar.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/Uli6xvic8BTCQfKXZuZt+/Screen+Shot+2025-10-03+at+10.27.51+PM.png)  
  

  2. Click the **+** button next to the default keyword (auto‑fetched from GBP).  
  

  3. Type your custom keyword (e.g., gym near me) and press Enter to add it.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/ergnGwHusG5N1NodcPr9+/GIF+Recording+2025-10-03+at+10.30.09+PM.gif)  
  


#### _**Step 4:** Configure Heatmap Parameters_

  


  1. Select **grid size** based on how dense you want your sampling points:  
  

     * **3×3** (9 points)
     * **5×5** (25 points)
     * **7×7** (49 points)  
  

  2. Set **coverage****distance** (radius from center to outermost marker): **1 km → 50 km**  
.
  3. Click on the **Run Local Search** button.  
  


    
    
    **Note:** You can generate up to **2 heatmaps** per month per prospect.
    

  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/n47FjXyyjQnOXM1zx9Is+/GIF+Recording+2025-10-03+at+10.35.11+PM.gif)

  
  


#### _**Step 5:** Explore Results and Competitors_

  


  1. Scroll to see the **Heatmap** displaying the keyword performance.  
  

  2. Each Pin on the map represents a **local****search** result from a **different****nearby****location**.  
  

  3. The **C****enter****Pin** reflects the **immediate****area** of the business.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/QQg9d0xWQXnNsxUCeQha+/Screen+Shot+2025-10-03+at+10.38.36+PM.png)  
  

  4. Click the **double‑arrow** icon to view the **Keyword Ranking Score card** \- summarizes average rank across all pins for a keyword.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/oESgGWT5Toku1EOI2MmQ+/Screen+Shot+2025-10-03+at+10.44.35+PM.png)  

  5. Under each pin, you’ll now see **10****competitors**.  
  

  6. Click any **business name** to open its Google Business Profile.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/IdtR2iRHWd5dKGKUAYgj+/GIF+Recording+2025-10-03+at+10.46.39+PM.gif)  
  


_**Step 6:** Control what Appears in Reports_

  


  1. If you’ve generated multiple heatmaps (e.g., default + custom keyword), **choose which heatmap** to **show** in the **reports**.  
  

  2. Only the selected heatmap is included in the **Share Report link and Export PDF**.

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

  


**Q: Can I run multiple heatmaps with different keywords simultaneously?**

Yes, you can generate multiple heatmaps with unique keyword, radius, and grid configurations side by side.  
  


**Q: What grid size options are available for the heatmap?**

You can choose between 3×3, 5×5, or 7×7 grid sizes, depending on how detailed you want your analysis to be.  
  


**Q: How do I adjust the coverage radius for a heatmap?**

The coverage radius can be set anywhere between 0.5 and 30 miles (1–50 km) to match the actual service area of the business.

  


**Q: Where is the Keyword Ranking Score displayed?**

The Keyword Ranking Score card appears as part of the heatmap interface, summarizing the average rank across all grid pins.

  


**Q: How do I decide which heatmaps to include in a prospect report?**

When sharing your report via Web URL or PDF, you can manually select the heatmaps that best highlight the prospect’s service areas and competitive standing.
