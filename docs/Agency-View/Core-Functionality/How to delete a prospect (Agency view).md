# How to delete a prospect (Agency view)

**Source URL:** [https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/155000000584-how-to-delete-a-prospect-agency-view-](https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/155000000584-how-to-delete-a-prospect-agency-view-)  
**Category:** Agency View  
**Folder:** Core Functionality

---

To delete a prospect from the prospecting tool, please head into your '**Agency View** ' > Click on '**Prospecting** ' > Search for the prospect you wish to '**delete** ' > Complete prompts (This step is irreversible)
