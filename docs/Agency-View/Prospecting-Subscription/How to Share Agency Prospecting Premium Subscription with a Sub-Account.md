# **What is the New Prospecting Tool Sharing Option with a Sub-Account?**

  


The new sharing option introduces a unified subscription model, allowing an agency having an active premium prospecting subscription to share its Premium subscription with one sub-account. This eliminates the need for duplicate subscriptions and offers more control over billing and access—all from a single place.

  


If Agencies don't want to share, they can also simply cancel agency level subscription and get a new subscription on any Sub-Account to being fresh prospecting in sub account (needs to be enabled under Reselling Tab in Agency View)

* * *

## **Key Benefits of the New Prospecting Tool Sharing Option**

  


Agencies can benefit from improved cost-efficiency and simpler workflows with this update:

  


  * **Cost Savings:** Avoid maintaining two separate subscriptions for agency and sub-account.  
  

  * **Simplified Subscription Management:** Manage billing and user access for both accounts in one place.  
  

  * **Full Feature Access:** Both the agency and the linked sub-account receive complete access to Prospecting Premium tools.  
  

  * **Operational Flexibility:** Adjust or change the linked sub-account as business needs evolve.

## **How To Share Agency Prospecting Premium Subscription with a Sub-Account**

  


Properly setting up your subscription and linking a sub-account ensures access to all features under a single billing structure.

  

    
    
    **Important:** This is a **one-time action**. You will not be able to change the sub-account later.  
    

  


### **_If you have already subscribed to Prospecting Premium at the agency level_**

  


  1. Click on **Prospecting** from your agency account.

# How to Remove or Update a Linked Sub-Account in the Agency Prospecting Tool: 

Each Agency Prospecting subscription can be linked to one sub-account. If you’d like to remove or update that linked sub-account, here’s how:

  


### To **remove the linked sub-account** (and not add another):

  1. ### 

Go to **Settings** and unsubscribe **either** at the **Agency** level or at the **linked sub-account** level.

  2. This will remove premium access for both.

  3. If you’d like to continue without linking a sub-account, simply start a new subscription **without linking any sub-account**.


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155053442062/original/zfhsbed4B-7n7qVNBX_XEcgeZo1UhyAKYQ.png?1757491339)

### To **replace the linked sub-account** with another:

You have two options:

  * **Option 1:**

    1. Go to **Settings** and unsubscribe at the **Agency** or at the **currently linked sub-account**.

    2. Start a new subscription and select the new sub-account to link.

  * **Option 2:**

    1. Cancel your existing subscription.

    2. Start a new subscription directly from the desired sub-account — either through **Reselling** or a **Direct Company Purchase** (explained below).

## **Upgrading to Prospecting Premium for the First Time:**

You have two ways to upgrade the **Prospecting Tool** to Premium:

  


## 1\. Agency-Level Prospecting

  * Includes all Premium benefits:

    * Unlimited Leads per month

    * Website Widgets

    * Leads POC Enrichment

    * Report Builder


**Important:** Agency-level Prospecting does _not_ include CRM features (e.g., Add to Contacts, Send SMS, Add to Opportunities, Workflow Triggers).

  * You will need to move into a **Sub-Account** later if you want CRM functionality.

  * **Recommendation:** Start with Prospecting inside a **Sub-Account** to access both Prospecting + CRM features right away.


## 2\. Sub-Account Level Prospecting

  * Includes **all Premium benefits** _plus_ CRM capabilities:

    * Add prospects to Contacts

    * Send SMS

    * Add to Opportunities

    * Trigger Workflows


### **How to Enable Prospecting in a Sub-Account**

You can enable it in three ways:

  1. **From the Reselling Tab**

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

  


**Q: Where can I link a sub-account to a Premium subscription?**  
This action can only be performed within the Agency Prospecting section.

  


**Q: Can I link more than one sub-account to my Prospecting Premium subscription?  
** No, only one sub-account can be linked per agency subscription.

  


**Q: Can I change the linked sub-account later?**  
No, linking a sub-account is irreversible. Once it’s linked, you won’t be able to change it.

  


**Q: Do both the agency and the sub-account get full access to Prospecting Premium?  
** Yes, both accounts receive complete access under a single subscription.

  


**Q: What if I want to subscribe without linking a sub-account?  
** You can choose to continue without linking during setup and link later from the subscription settings.

  


**Q: What happens if the selected sub-account already has an active Premium subscription?**  
If the sub-account already has an active Premium subscription, we’ll automatically adjust your billing and cancel any duplicate subscription to prevent extra charges.
