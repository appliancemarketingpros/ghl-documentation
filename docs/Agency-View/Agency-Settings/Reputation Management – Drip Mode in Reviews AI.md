# **What is Drip Mode in Reviews AI?**

  
Drip Mode applies pacing to automated review replies so historical reviews receive thoughtful, on-brand responses over time rather than all at once. By controlling cadence, windows, and eligibility rules, teams can safely catch up on backlogs without triggering spam-like activity.

  
Drip Mode is a configurable campaign inside Reviews AI that targets older, unreplied reviews and schedules AI-generated responses over a defined cadence and time window. Campaigns can be paused, edited, or deleted at any time.

* * *

## **Key Benefits of Drip Mode**

  
Understanding the benefits clarifies when to use Drip Mode and how it advances your reputation strategy beyond one-off replies.  
  


  * **Backlog Cleanup:** Address weeks, months, or years of unanswered reviews without manual effort.  
  

  * **Human-like Pacing:** Avoid sudden bursts by setting daily caps, cadences, and allowed send windows.  
  

  * **B****rand Consistency:** Use Reviews AI agents and tone settings for contextual, on-brand replies.  
  

  * **Operational Control:** Pause, edit, or delete campaigns while preserving sent history.  
  

  * **Improved Trust & Response Rates:** Increase reply coverage without appearing automated or spammy.


* * *

## **Campaign Management Controls****  
**

  
Lifecycle controls let you adapt to changing priorities without losing historical context or sent history.  
  


  * **Edit Campaign:** Update configuration (age, caps, cadence, windows, agent). Changes apply to future scheduled replies and do not alter already-sent responses.  
  

  * **Pause Campaign:** Temporarily stop future replies while keeping settings intact for later resumption.  
  

  * **Delete Campaign:** Cancel all pending replies and permanently remove the campaign. Sent replies remain in history.


* * *

## **Monitoring & Reporting****  
**

  
Visibility into scheduled and sent replies ensures accountability, helps forecast backlog clearance, and supports QA.

  


  * **Scheduled Queue:** Review upcoming replies, counts remaining for the day, and next send window.  
  

  * **Sent History:** Track which reviews were replied to, timestamps, and response content.  
  

  * **Backlog Progress:** Monitor how many eligible reviews remain and estimated days to completion based on caps.  
  

  * **Failures & Retries: **Identify any failed sends and take corrective action where available.

## **Limitations & Notes****  
**

  
Knowing the boundaries helps you plan realistic timelines and avoid unexpected behavior.  
  


  * Drip Mode targets historical reviews based on your minimum age setting and unreplied filter.  
  

  * Edits affect only future scheduled replies. Already-sent replies remain unchanged.  
  

  * Pausing halts future sends until resumed; deleting removes pending items.  
  

  * Actual send timing depends on your cadence, time windows, and daily caps.

## **Frequently Asked Questions****  
**

  
**Q: Can I run more than one Drip Mode campaign at the same time?  
** Yes, if supported by your account/location setup. Ensure campaigns target distinct review sets to avoid overlap.  
  


**Q: How are historical reviews selected for reply?  
** Eligible reviews meet your minimum age and unreplied-only filters. They’re added to a schedule and sent according to your cadence and time window.  
  


**Q: What happens when the daily cap is reached?  
** Remaining eligible reviews queue to the next available send window based on your cadence.  
  


**Q: Do edits affect already-sent replies?  
** No. Edits only change future scheduled replies.  
  


**Q: Can I pause the campaign without losing my settings?  
** Yes. Pausing preserves configuration and halts future sends until you resume.  
  


**Q: What’s the best way to avoid replies sounding repetitive?  
** Use appropriate tone settings, include brand context, and periodically review samples from the Sent history to refine agent/tone.  
  


**Q: How do I avoid conflicts with auto-replies for new reviews?  
** Keep Drip Mode focused on older reviews and ensure your new-review automation remains separate.  
  


**Q: Can I restrict replies to certain hours or days?  
** Yes. Use Allowed Time Window and your chosen cadence to keep replies within business hours.  
  


**Q: Does Drip Mode handle negative reviews differently?  
** Tone and agent selection can emphasize empathy and resolution language, helping address sensitive feedback professionally.  
  


**Q: Where do I see failures or retry items?  
** Use the monitoring views to check for failed sends and take corrective action if options are available in your account.
