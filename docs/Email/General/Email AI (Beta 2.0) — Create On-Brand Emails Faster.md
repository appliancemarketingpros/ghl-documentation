Email AI (Beta 2.0)

Generate on-brand emails in minutes with guided prompts, Brand Board styling, and conversational edits.

Labs Feature

This feature is currently in Labs. To enable it, go to **Settings → Labs** and toggle **Email AI (Beta 2.0)** on for the desired sub-accounts.

Overview

Email AI is a guided, brand-aware experience inside the Email Marketing builder that helps you plan, draft, and design emails quickly. It leverages your Brand Board for fonts and colors, recommends layouts, and lets you refine content conversationally — so you can launch emails faster with more control and less rework.

## Limits & Known Behaviors (Beta)

Reference Fidelity

Complex custom code, tracking pixels, or scripts from the reference won't carry over. AI recreates an editable layout instead.

Assets

Replace any sample images with licensed assets and confirm link targets after generation.

Brand Board Changes

Updating your default Brand Board affects future generations only. Previously generated drafts remain unchanged unless edited.

Tip

For pixel-perfect vendor HTML or advanced custom code, import it into the Code Editor first. Then use Email AI in the visual builder to create new branded variations.

## How To Set Up and Use Email AI

Follow these steps to enable access, prepare your brand styles, and generate your first on-brand draft.

Prerequisites

**Email Marketing access** — an account with Email Marketing enabled.

**Brand Board (recommended)** — a configured Brand Board for best styling results.

**Labs permissions** — appropriate role permissions to enable Labs features (if required in your account).

Setup Guide

Create with Email AI

From blank prompt to on-brand draft in a few clicks.

Step 1

Open Email Marketing Templates

Navigate to **Email Marketing → Emails** , then click the **Templates** tab.

Step 2

Launch Email AI

Click **Build with AI**. Alternatively, open an existing campaign or template and select the AI option in edit mode.

![Build with AI button in the Templates tab](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155065199293/original/uZ2rtDWlz88D37xMqkjNzT4nX5I2Jv-oHA.png?1771430121)

Step 3

Enable Assist Mode and confirm your Brand Board

Click **Assist Mode** and verify the correct Brand Board is selected so generated emails use your colors and typography.

![Assist Mode and Brand Board confirmation screen](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155065199586/original/t2TUFxyNHgWnoFnRNXD0PwHQAA9T__J88w.png?1771430247)

Step 4

Choose a starting method

Pick one of two approaches:

  * **From a Reference** — upload a file or image to recreate a layout you already like.
  * **From a Prompt** — describe your audience, goal, offer, tone, and desired sections.


![Starting method selection — From a Reference or From a Prompt](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155065199758/original/_dvFllT1fusI0CweyYDWy4JeEYeZAWhaYw.png?1771430359)

Step 5

Generate and iterate conversationally

After the draft is generated, refine it using plain-language commands: **Rewrite** , **Shorten** , **Translate** , **Add Section** , or **Change Theme**.

![Conversational editing commands in Email AI](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155065200495/original/3nQJp7nPGNFj5SajHPLmcSUNDYQwBJCpYw.gif?1771430724)

Step 6

Preview and finalize

Preview all links, images, and compliance content. Make any final manual edits directly in the builder.

![Previewing the email in the builder](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155065200657/original/p5-0gqFVVt2itTSV1uOrWfyT3IiQx0jZjQ.gif?1771430847)

Step 7

Review version history and save

Open **Version History** from the Kebab Menu to compare and restore earlier generations. When ready, click **Save Template** to schedule or send in your campaign flow.

![Version History option in the Kebab menu](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155065200808/original/xiuVrEIyVQNWxhKkVp4bu2nE6D8X9eXFFw.png?1771430935)

You're All Set

Your email is ready to schedule or send. Return to Version History any time to restore a previous generation.

## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Email AI available to all users or only specific roles?

Availability is managed via Labs by admins. If you don't see Email AI, ask an Agency Admin to enable the **Email AI (Beta 2.0)** toggle for your sub-account under **Settings → Labs**.

Q: What types of references work best for "Start from a reference"?

Public preview URLs and clear full-page screenshots (PNG/JPG) yield the best structure. Avoid partial crops or pages with heavy custom scripting.

Q: Will Email AI copy exact HTML/CSS from a referenced email?

No. It recreates an editable layout aligned to your brand. For pixel-perfect reproduction, use the **Code Editor** import workflow instead.

Q: How does the Brand Board influence the generated email?

Brand colors, typography, and common style tokens apply to headings, body text, buttons, and links. You can override styles in the editor or via AI commands at any time.

Q: Can I translate emails with Email AI?

Yes. Use conversational commands like "Translate to Spanish." Always have a native speaker review translations for nuance before sending.

Q: Where can I access previous versions of a generated email?

Open **Version History** from the Kebab Menu in the Email Editor to review, compare, and restore earlier generations.

Q: Does changing my default Brand Board restyle existing emails?

No. Brand Board changes affect new generations only. Edit existing templates manually if you want updated branding applied.

Q: What's the difference between Email AI and AI in Workflows?

Email AI lives in **Email Marketing → Templates/Editor** and is designed for creating and designing full email messages. Workflow AI generates copy inside automation steps (Send Email action) and operates in a different context with different capabilities.
