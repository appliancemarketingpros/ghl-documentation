## **Overview**

We've introduced a new agency-level setting that gives you centralized control over Email Services across all your sub-accounts. This update improves governance, simplifies management, and makes email configuration easier to find and oversee from one place.

##   
**What's New**

A new toggle has been added under:

**Agency View → Settings → Email Services → Advanced Settings**

This toggle controls whether the **"Add Email Service"** button is visible and available within sub-accounts. Agencies can now enable or disable access to Email Services for sub-accounts directly from this centralized setting — no need to manage it account by account.

### **For New Agencies**

  * The Email Services control toggle is **enabled by default**
  * The **"Add Services"** button will be **hidden** in sub-accounts
  * Sub-account access to Email Services is governed entirely by the agency-level setting


### **For Existing Agencies**

  * The Email Services control toggle is **disabled by default**
  * The **"Add Services"** button **remains visible** in sub-accounts, preserving your current workflow
  * No disruption to any sub-accounts already using Email Services


> **No action is required** for existing sub-accounts that are already set up with Email Services. Their configurations will continue to work as expected.

##   


## **Managing Email Services for a Specific Sub-Account**

If you want to enable or disable the "Add Email Service" button for an individual sub-account rather than applying the setting globally, you can do so at the sub-account level:

  1. Go to **Agency View → Sub-Accounts**
  2. Select the sub-account you want to configure
  3. Navigate to **Advanced Settings → Disable the "Add Email Service" button in the sub-account Email Service Settings**
  4. Toggle the setting on or off based on your preference for that sub-account

##   
**Key Benefits**

**Centralized Governance** : Manage Email Services availability across all sub-accounts from a single agency-level toggle, rather than handling it individually per account.

**Better Discoverability** :All email-related controls are now organized under Email Services in Agency Settings, making it easier to find and manage configurations in one place.

**Flexible Overrides** — Sub-accounts can still respect and, where applicable, override the agency-level configuration to accommodate unique needs.

##   
**Frequently Asked Questions**

**Will this affect my existing sub-accounts that are already using Email Services?** No. Existing sub-accounts with Email Services already configured are not affected. This change only governs the visibility of the "Add Services" button going forward.

**I'm an existing agency. Do I need to do anything?** No action is required. The toggle defaults to disabled for existing agencies, so your current setup remains unchanged.

**Can sub-accounts still manage their own Email Services settings?** Yes. Sub-accounts can still respect and override the agency-level configuration where applicable.

**Where do I find this new setting?** Navigate to **Agency View → Settings → Email Services → Advanced Settings** to find the new toggle.
