# **What is Company Export?**

  
Company Export is a HighLevel feature that downloads company records from **Contacts → Companies** as a **CSV file**. Exporting to CSV helps you work with company data in tools like Excel or Google Sheets while keeping the information organized in a consistent spreadsheet format.

* * *

## **Key Benefits of Company Export**

  
Having a fast, repeatable way to pull company data improves reporting and reduces manual work—especially in B2B setups where companies are a core part of the CRM.

  


  * **Bulk exporting:** Download multiple company records at once instead of copying details record-by-record.  
  


  * **Shareable format:** Use CSV to share company data with teammates or external partners in a standard spreadsheet format.  
  


  * **Offline analysis:** Review, filter, and sort exported company data in spreadsheet tools for audits or reporting.  
  


  * **Flexible workflows:** Export either a targeted set of companies or a complete list depending on your goal.


* * *

## **Export Options**

  
Understanding the two export paths helps you choose the quickest workflow based on whether you need a subset of records or the full dataset.

  


  * **Export selected companies (from the table):** Select one or more companies using checkboxes, then export from the action bar.  
  


  * **Export all companies (from the menu):** Use the **three-dot (⋮) menu** on the Companies page to export the full list.


* * *

## **Real-Time Export Progress Modal**

  
A progress modal helps you track export generation so you can confidently export large datasets without guessing when the file is ready.

  


During an export, HighLevel displays a progress indicator that shows:

  


  * **Record count** (how many records have been processed)  
  


  * **Percent complete** (overall completion progress)


* * *

## **CSV File Structure & Fields**

  
Knowing what to expect in the CSV helps you prepare for reporting, cleanup, or importing into another system.

  


  * The export is delivered as a **CSV** (comma-separated values) file.  
  


  * The CSV includes **company record data** in columns so it can be filtered, sorted, and reviewed in spreadsheet tools.  
  


  * If your account uses **company custom fields** , verify whether those fields appear as columns in your export (recommended before relying on the file for downstream reporting).

## **How To Setup Company Export**

  
Exporting does not require setup, but following a consistent export process helps ensure you download the right records (especially when filters are applied).

  


### **Export selected companies**

  


  1. Go to **Contacts → Companies**. (Optional) Use **search or filters** to narrow the list.  
  
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155065757930/original/tHGZod3ZIC8t0im023IJoKCDIZx9OXNqbA.png?1772099199)  
  


  2. Select one or more companies using the **checkboxes** in the table. Click **Export** in the action bar.  
  
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155065758182/original/8q2mBIQhtKzWx3hh8jMhGwbjGi6ZnujgRA.png?1772099276)  
  


  3. Watch the **progress modal** until the export completes.  
  
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155065758395/original/23cgH8YQfS9Ecwdrck42I8dyqFqT7mEkJw.png?1772099303)  
  


  4. Download/open the CSV file when it becomes available.  
  


### **Export all companies**

  


  1. Go to **Contacts → Companies**. Click the **three-dot (⋮) menu** in the top-right and Select **Export**.

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

  


**Q: Can I export only a few companies instead of the entire list?**  
Yes. Select the companies using the checkboxes in **Contacts → Companies** , then export from the action bar.  
  


**Q: Does the export include custom company fields?**  
Yes. All visible company fields, including custom fields, are included in the export.

  


**Q: What file type is generated?**  
The export is provided as a **CSV** file.  
  


**Q: My team member can’t see the Export option, why?**  
Their user role may not have export access for company data. Review their [role permissions](<https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/155000002544-user-roles-permissions-and-assigned-data-subaccount>) and ensure they have access to Companies and any related export/download permissions.
