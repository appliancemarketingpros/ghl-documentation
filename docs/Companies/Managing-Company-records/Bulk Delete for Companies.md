# What is Bulk Delete for Companies?

Bulk Delete is a list-view action on Companies that lets you select multiple records using checkboxes and delete them in one go.

You can select records individually, select all records on a page, or apply filters and search first and then select from the filtered results.

#   


# Key benefits

  * Remove multiple Companies in a single action.
  * Clean up Companies directly from the list view without opening each one.
  * Consistent select-and-delete experience across Companies, Custom Objects, and Tasks.
  * Recover deleted Companies within 60 days from the Bulk Actions page.


#   


# How to bulk delete Companies

2\. On the list view, use the checkboxes on the left of each row to select the Companies you want to delete. Use the header checkbox to select all records on the page.

# What happens when a Company is deleted

When you delete a Company, the Company record and all of its field values are removed.

All relations and associations on the deleted Company are also removed, including:

  * Associations to related **Contacts** , **Opportunities** , and **Custom Object records**
  * Associations to related **Notes** and **Tasks**
  * Any **association labels** applied to those relationships


Existing cascade behavior for Companies applies to any directly owned data on the Company record.

#   


# How to recover deleted Companies

Bulk-deleted Companies can be restored within **60 days** of deletion from the **Bulk Actions** page. After 60 days, the Companies are permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

To restore deleted Companies:

  


1\. Go to **Bulk Actions** in your sub-account.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155071006133/original/gWdQ6AvsALhJGTUzXFxxz1nulpiLvFIEFA.png?1778490826)

  


2\. Locate the relevant job row and click the **three-dot menu** on the right. Click **Restore**.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155071006194/original/g2szJw1gcyEKINwPMluOGe7F0w6sosm8Yg.png?1778490870)

  


3\. Confirm the action in the dialog.

  


The restore runs as a new job and will appear in the Bulk Actions table. Once it completes, the Companies will be back on the list view along with their field values and associations.

# Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I bulk delete Companies from a filtered or searched list view?**

Yes. Apply your filters or search first, then use the checkboxes to select records from the filtered results.

  


**Q: How long do I have to restore deleted Companies?**

60 days from the date of deletion. After that, the Companies are permanently removed.

  


**Q: Are associations restored when I restore a deleted Company?**

Yes. When you restore a Company from the Bulk Actions page, its previous associations and association labels are restored along with the record.
