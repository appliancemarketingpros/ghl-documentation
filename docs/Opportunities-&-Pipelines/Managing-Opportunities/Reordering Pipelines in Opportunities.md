# Reordering Pipelines in Opportunities

**Source URL:** [https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/155000008080-reordering-pipelines-in-opportunities](https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/155000008080-reordering-pipelines-in-opportunities)  
**Category:** Opportunities & Pipelines  
**Folder:** Managing Opportunities

---

You can arrange your pipelines in the order that works best for you, right from the Opportunities pipeline view. Reorder by dragging, or move a pipeline to an exact position. Your custom order is saved automatically and stays in place when you refresh the page.

  
**TABLE OF CONTENTS**

  


  * Reorder by drag and drop
  * Move a pipeline to a specific position
  * Good to know


## Reorder by drag and drop

  1. Go to **Opportunities** and open the **Pipelines** tab.
  2. Hover over the drag handle (the dotted icon) on the left of any pipeline row.
  3. Drag the pipeline up or down to the position you want, then release.

![Dragging a pipeline to a new position in the Pipelines list](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155073472806/original/Tqtv9kpzrPj2bm_NhWTCb__8RzjPP5th-w.png?1781185020)Drag a pipeline by its handle to reorder it.

## Move a pipeline to a specific position

Use this option when you want to place a pipeline at an exact spot. It also works across pages, so you can reposition a pipeline even if it isn't on the page you're currently viewing.

  1. On the pipeline row, click the **⋯ (three-dot) Actions** menu.
  2. Select **Move to position**.

![The three-dot Actions menu with Move to position highlighted](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155073472807/original/86l1JNPREH6Z_oKFRXp_a2dBRDnblAOyYQ.png?1781185020)Open the Actions menu and choose Move to position.

  1. In the **Reorder Pipeline** dialog, enter the position number you want.
  2. Click **Apply**.

![The Reorder Pipeline dialog with a Move to position field](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155073472805/original/ZJKnO2kbz68NbrraUQFV9vpX4eWGXsrRgw.png?1781185020)Enter the target position and click Apply.

The pipeline moves to that position and the list renumbers automatically.

![The Pipelines list showing the updated order](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155073472808/original/FQXvC1zo6VxUPQr2-2q84grr69VZxcraBw.png?1781185020)The list reflects the new order right away.

## Good to know

  * Your custom order is saved automatically — there is no separate Save button.
  * The order persists across page refreshes.
  * **Move to position** supports moving a pipeline across pages, which is useful when you manage many pipelines.
  * This feature is available only for admins and the setting is saved across the location for all users.
