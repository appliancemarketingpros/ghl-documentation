## **Troubleshoot 3D Secure (3DS) Card Authentication During Trial Signup**

  


Some banks require **3D Secure (3DS)** authentication (often an OTP) to verify cards online. This is common for cards issued in India. If your trial signup fails during payment, use the 3DS-supported signup flow below and complete the authentication step.

  


## **Use the 3DS-supported signup link**

  


1\. Open: <https://affiliates.gohighlevel.com>  
  


2\. Start your trial signup and enter your card details.  
  


3\. If prompted, complete your bank’s **3DS/OTP authentication**.

  
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155064182183/original/RZk1EvbEtcU7zWE5Fsdi7AI2m8wP-nBb1A.png?1770211459)  


This signup flow is designed to trigger 3DS when required and validate the card verification securely.

  


### **What you should see**

  * A bank-authentication step (OTP/approval) if your card requires 3DS.
  * Signup completes after you approve the authentication.


  


### **About the $1 authorization**

To trigger 3DS when required and verify your card, the signup flow may place a **$1 authorization** on your card.

  


  * This is **not a charge** for a plan or subscription.
  * It can appear as a **temporary** pending authorization and should disappear based on your bank’s processing time.


  


### **If authentication still fails**

  * Try again in an up-to-date browser.
  * Disable ad blockers for the signup page.
  * Allow pop-ups/redirects if your browser blocks the bank authentication page.
  * Try another card if your bank declines the verification.


  


**Note:** HighLevel is working to bring the same 3DS-supported experience to the main signup flow. Until then, use the signup link above if you experience 3DS-related failures.
