# **What is the HighLevel Affiliate Program?**

  


The HighLevel Affiliate Program allows you to share HighLevel with your audience and earn commissions when referrals become paying customers. Whether you're new to HighLevel or you're a passionate veteran, the HighLevel Affiliate program is a great way to earn 40% monthly recurring commission from everyone you introduce to HighLevel!

  


If you haven't signed up to be an Affiliate yet, click on the [link here](<https://www.gohighlevel.com/joinaffiliate>). 

* * *

## **Key Benefits of Landing HighLevel Affiliates**

  


  * **40% lifetime commissions:** earn 40% recurring commissions on paid HighLevel accounts you directly refer (per campaign terms).  
  

  * **Compounding recurring income:** commissions accrue as long as referred accounts remain active, creating predictable, MRR‑like cash flow.  
  

  * **No fulfillment or support burden:** HighLevel manages the product, billing, and support—freeing you to focus on audience building and promotion.  
  

  * **Transparent tracking & insights:** the Affiliate Portal shows clicks, trials, customers, downline activity (where applicable), and commissions so you can optimize what works.

## **How to Find Your First 5 Affiliate Referrals**

  


For all of our current affiliates looking to get their first five affiliates, we met with our top affiliates and here's their advice to you!

  


### _**Method 1:** Send An Email/SMS To Your Network_

  


Word‑of‑mouth is still the most effective marketing. If you love HighLevel, odds are your colleagues will, too. Every HighLevel account has access to the **Marketing Agency Snapshot** , which includes a starter **Potential Affiliate Nurture** you can customize.

  


  * Create a sub‑account using the **[Marketing Agency Snapshot](<https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/48001079550>)**.  
  

  * Go to **Marketing** > **Email >****Campaigns** > **A Potential Affiliate Nurture** and tailor the copy for your voice.  
  

  * **Activate** the campaign.  
  

  * Go to **Contacts > ****Smart-lists** , select contacts from your network who can benefit from using HighLevel, and use **Add To Campaign** to enroll them.


  


### _**Method 2:** Create Educational YouTube Videos_

  


Why did you switch to HighLevel? What are your favorite features? What specific workflows helped you level up? Turn those answers into short screen‑recorded videos.

  


  * Keep titles outcome‑focused (e.g., “Launch a Lead‑Gen Funnel in Minutes with HighLevel”).  
  

  * Place your **affiliate****link** at the top of the video description and pin it in the first comment.  
  

  * Add timestamps for key moments to improve retention and clicks.


  


### _**Method 3:** Write Insightful Blog Posts_

  


Agency owners actively research tools. Publish posts like “[**Previous Tool] vs HighLevel — Why I Made The Switch** ” or “**[Other Tool 1] vs [Other Tool 2] — Why I Left Both.** ”  
  


  * Add your affiliate link near the top (contextual) and again as a clear CTA at the end.  
  

  * Follow SEO best practices so your post indexes and surfaces for relevant searches.


  


### _**Method 4:** Hunt Down Opportunities on Social Media_

  


Use social listening to find real problems you can solve with HighLevel.

  


  * Search competitor names, feature keywords, and hashtags to find conversations.  
  

  * Join the thread with a concise tip from your experience and invite a **DM** or share your affiliate link for a trial.  
  

  * Keep messages short and helpful; tailor the benefit to their exact pain (saving time, saving money, or getting better results).


  


### _**Method 5:** Start Building a Community_

  

    
    
    **Note:** Make sure to join the [HighLevel Affiliate Community](<https://affiliatecommunity.gohighlevel.com/communities/groups/highlevel-affiliate-community/private-group>), to build connections with other HighLevel affiliates, receive Affiliate related announcements, ask your burning affiliate related questions, and so much more!  

  


Communities (Facebook Groups, Circle, etc.) are a great place to cultivate relationships with potential affiliates and customers. Share wins and processes to build trust and engagement.  
  


  * Document your workflows and post quick wins with a CTA like: “**Join our XYZ Group for how we made this happen.** ”  
  

  * Link your affiliate page in the group description and welcome post.

**Q. Where do I find my affiliate link?**

From your **agency** **account** go to the **Affiliate** **Portal** , copy your referral URL under Dashboard.   
  


**Q. Can I create multiple affiliate campaigns for different offers?**
