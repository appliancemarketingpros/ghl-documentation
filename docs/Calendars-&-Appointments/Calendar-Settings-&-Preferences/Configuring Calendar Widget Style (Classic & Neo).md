# **What is the Calendar Widget Style?**

  


Calendar widget styles define how your booking calendar appears when shared with leads or clients. HighLevel offers the Classic widget and the Neo widget, each designed for different use cases and levels of customization.

* * *

## **Key Benefits of Calendar Widget Styles**

  


Understanding the benefits of each widget style helps you choose the right one for your scheduling needs.

  


  * **Classic Style** : Simple, reliable layout designed for quick setup and basic appointment booking.


  


  * **Neo Style** : Modern, customizable interface with advanced features like widget step reordering, color customization, staff selection, and class seat displays.


  


  * **Flexibility** : Ability to switch between styles when creating or editing a calendar.


  


  * **Default for Group Calendars** : The Neo widget is automatically applied for Group Calendars, offering a sleek group booking experience.


* * *

## **Classic Widget**

  


The Classic widget is designed for straightforward appointment booking with a simple layout. It’s best for businesses that don’t need advanced customization or modern design elements.

  


  * Quick setup process


  


  * Standard layout for single or unassigned calendars


  


  * Ideal for businesses wanting minimal customization

## **Neo Widget**

  


The Neo widget is the newer, modern style designed with customization and flexibility in mind. It’s the default style for Group Calendars and supports enhanced features.

  


  * Customizable colors, backgrounds, and buttons


  


  * Ability to reorder widget steps (Form, Date & Time, Payment)


  


  * Staff selection option for Round Robin calendars


  


  * Seat display for Class Booking calendars


  


  * Streamlined and modern interface

## **How To Setup Calendar Widget Styles**

  


Choosing or changing your calendar widget style ensures that your booking page matches your preferred design and features.

  


  

    
    
    **Classic vs Neo: Things to Keep in Mind.**
    
    It’s important to be aware of how Classic and Neo widgets differ so you can choose the right option for your calendar setup. 
    
    Classic is the default for unassigned calendars, while Neo is the default for group calendars. Customization options such as colors, backgrounds, and button text are only available in Neo. 
    
    Staff selection in Round Robin calendars also requires Neo, and the Class Booking seat display is only supported in Neo. 
    
    Payment collection, however, is supported in both Classic and Neo. 
    
    You can switch widget styles anytime without affecting existing bookings.

  


  


### **Open Settings**

  


Begin by navigating to the **Settings** menu from the left sidebar. This is where you can manage your account preferences, business details, and calendar configurations.

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155053389542/original/nWbGf1844DFD6LZ2BZ-cdxkMBKzdyR8LfQ.png?1757424497)

  


  


### **Access Calendars**

  


Inside the Settings menu, click on **Calendars** under the Business Services section. This will take you to the calendar management area where all your existing calendars are listed.

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155053389596/original/R8zGPM6caJtwmbcZnsyILto3USKu-aQ3-A.png?1757424537)

  


  


### **Create or Edit Calendar**

  


In the Calendars section, you can either click **New Calendar** to create a fresh one or use the **edit icon** next to an existing calendar to update its settings. This is where you’ll set or adjust the widget style.

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155053389669/original/knlBJ5svshub779YYyQQtSglHMRj5T1NGA.png?1757424579)

### **Select Widget Style**

  


Go to the **Customizations** tab in the calendar setup. Under **Calendar Widget Style** , choose between the Classic or Neo design. Neo is the default for group calendars, while Classic is the default for unassigned calendars.

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

  


**Q: Can I switch from Classic to Neo after creating a calendar?**  
Yes, you can edit an existing calendar at any time and change the widget style.

  


**Q: Do all customization options apply to both Classic and Neo widgets?**  
No, customization features such as colors, backgrounds, and reorder options are exclusive to the Neo widget.

  


**Q: Why is Neo the default for Group Calendars?**  
Neo supports advanced group booking features, staff selection, and a modern layout better suited for multiple team members.

  


**Q: Which widget should I use for class bookings with multiple seats?**  
The Neo widget is required to display available seats per slot.

  


**Q: Does switching widget styles affect existing bookings?**  
No, changing the widget style only changes the booking page appearance, not your existing appointments.
