# **What is Calendar View Enhancements?**

  


The Calendar View Enhancements introduce a modernized scheduling experience designed to reduce clutter and adapt dynamically to your workflow. Depending on your scheduling type—**Meetings** , **Services (v2)** , or upcoming **Rentals** —the view adjusts filters, displays, and navigation tools so you can organize appointments effortlessly.

  


_This update focuses on improving usability by introducing type-specific calendar views, streamlined filters, and flexible viewing options that expand screen space for better visibility._

## **Key Benefits of Calendar View Enhancements**

  


Understanding the main advantages helps you maximize productivity and simplify daily scheduling.  
  


  * **Focused views:** Only appointments and filters related to your active scheduling type are shown—no more mixed results or visual clutter.  
  


  * **Smart navigation:** A new dropdown lets you switch between _Meetings_ and _Services_ instantly.  
  


  * **Optimized workspace:** Use the **Manage View** toggle to hide filters and expand your visible calendar area.  
  


  * **Quick monthly navigation:** The mini month-view has been relocated under the main date for easy access.  
  


  * **Cleaner interface:** If your agency has only one scheduling type enabled, the dropdown hides automatically for a distraction-free workspace.  
  


  * **Unified Service View:** Displays multi-staff service bookings clearly in one view.


* * *

## **Dedicated Scheduling Types**

  


HighLevel’s Calendar View now recognizes separate scheduling types to better align with your business model and booking needs.

  


_This feature ensures that each type—Meetings, Services, or Rentals—has its own focused workspace, tailored filters, and dedicated management tools._

  * **Meetings:** Includes all legacy calendar types—Personal, Round Robin, Collective, Class, Service Menu, and Service Calendars (v1).  
  


  * **Services (v2):** Built for service-based businesses. Supports advanced capabilities like **variations** , **add-ons** , and **staff pricing**.  
  


  * **Rentals (coming soon):** A future release designed for rental-based businesses (e.g., studios, venues, or equipment rentals).

## **Smart Dropdown Navigation**

  


The new **Smart Dropdown** makes switching between scheduling types effortless. It appears at the top of the calendar when multiple scheduling types are enabled in **Agency Settings**. If only one type is active, the dropdown stays hidden, ensuring a cleaner layout.

  


_This contextual navigation improves workflow continuity—no more switching tabs or sub-accounts to view different calendar types._

## **Unified Service View**

  
When toggled on, **Unified Service View** groups all service-based bookings (across staff) in one unified timeline, providing complete visibility of your service schedule at a glance.

  
_This view helps service-based teams quickly identify overlaps, staff assignments, and available time slots across multiple locations._

## **More Space to View Events**

  


To maximize visibility, the enhanced Calendar View reduces padding and allows you to hide the filter panel.

  


_This makes it ideal for users who handle a high volume of appointments and need to see as many events as possible without scrolling._

  


**How to expand view space:**

  * Click **Manage View** (top right corner).

## **Calendar View Enhancements Availability**  
  


The enhanced **Calendar View** feature is automatically available when **Meetings** , **Services (v2)** , or both scheduling types are enabled within your agency settings. Once active, the calendar interface updates automatically—no additional setup is required.

  
When multiple scheduling types are enabled, a **type selector** appears at the top of the Calendar View, allowing users to switch between **Meetings** and **Services**. If only one scheduling type is active, the selector remains hidden to maintain a simplified layout.

  
The **Manage View** panel on the right includes dynamic filters that adjust based on the selected scheduling type. This ensures that users only see relevant filters, such as **Users** , **Calendars** , or **Groups** for Meetings, and **Staff** , **Services** , or **Categories** for Services. Agencies offering service-based bookings can also use **Unified Service View** to view all service appointments across team members in a single, consolidated timeline.

  
The updated layout is designed for greater visibility and flexibility. The filter panel can be collapsed to display more appointments on screen, and a **mini month calendar** positioned beneath the main date header allows for quick date navigation without leaving the current view.

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

  


**Q: Do I need to migrate my existing calendars to use the new view?**

A: No. All existing calendars automatically appear under the **Meetings** view. **Services (v2)** calendars show in the **Services** view once that feature is enabled.

  


**Q: Why don’t I see the Meetings/Services dropdown?**

A: The dropdown only appears if multiple scheduling types are active in **Agency Settings**.

  


**Q: Can I still drag and drop appointments?**

A: Yes, you can still drag, drop, reschedule, and cancel appointments as before.

  


**Q: Does hiding the filter panel affect other users?**

A: No. The **Manage View** setting is user-specific and does not impact others.

  


**Q: Will Google Calendar sync with Services (v2)?**

A: Not currently. Google Calendar sync applies to Meetings only; Services must be managed directly within HighLevel.

  


**Q: Is Rentals available yet?**

A: Rentals are under development and will be added in a future update.

  


**Q: Can I enable Services (v2) for multiple sub-accounts at once?**

A: Yes. Go to **Agency Settings → Subaccount → Bulk Actions → Setup Calendars** to enable Services across accounts.
