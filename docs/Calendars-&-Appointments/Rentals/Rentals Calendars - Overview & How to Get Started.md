# **What are Rentals?**

  


Welcome to **Rentals** , our new scheduling and booking system designed for businesses that manage rentals — whether it’s properties, cars, equipment, or other bookable assets. With Rentals, you can efficiently manage availability, inventory, pricing, and multi-day bookings in one seamless platform.

## **What's New in Rentals**

  


Rentals is built to handle the unique complexities of rental-based scheduling. Here’s what’s Rentals can do:

  


  


  * #### **Multi-Day Booking Support:** Book assets across multiple hours, days, weeks, or months, with flexible duration and booking controls.  
  


  * #### **Listings and Categories:[(Help Guide)](<https://help.gohighlevel.com/en/support/solutions/articles/155000006573>) **Create listings for your rentals with detailed information, organized categories, and status management.  
  


  * #### **Inventory & Variants: [(Help Guide)](<https://help.gohighlevel.com/en/support/solutions/articles/155000006579>) **Enable quantity tracking for multiple units and define variants for different versions of the same listing.  
  


  * #### **Advanced Pricing & Discounts: [(Help Guide)](<https://help.gohighlevel.com/en/support/solutions/articles/155000006611>) **Use pricing rules for seasons, days of the week, hour of day, durations, or quantities. Automatically apply discounts for long-term or bulk bookings.  
  


  * #### **Calendar View:[(Help Guide)](<https://help.gohighlevel.com/en/support/solutions/articles/155000006641>) **View all bookings visually using a modern, filterable calendar that supports day, week, and month views. Identify overlaps, blocked slots, and real-time availability.  
  


  * #### **External Calendar Sync:** Connect and sync bookings with third party platforms using Calendar Sync to ensure your availability is consistent across platforms.  
  


  * #### **Centralized Global Settings:[(Help Guide)](<https://help.gohighlevel.com/en/support/solutions/articles/155000006640>) **Control business operating hours, booking defaults, payment preferences, and communication templates from one location.  
  


  * #### **Built-in Payments and Invoicing:** Collect payments directly from the booking flow and generate invoices automatically for each confirmed booking. Setup security deposits per your business needs.  
  


  * #### **Customer Booking Page:** Offer customers a streamlined booking experience with a dedicated public-facing booking page that displays your listings, categories, pricing and availability in real-time.

## **Setting Up Rentals**

  


If you don't see the Rentals tab in your Calendar settings, enable it by following the instructions in [How To Enable Rentals in Public Beta](<https://help.gohighlevel.com/en/support/solutions/articles/155000006740>).

  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155067391480/original/RNLREwxM5todPYoOPkwNW7ribzNukUrqcA.png?1774029055)

  


Once Rentals is enabled for your sub-account, continue:

  


#### **_Step 1:_**_Access Rentals_

  
Go to **Calendars → Calendar Settings → Rentals**

  


**![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155056178317/original/XU-M2Nj6rWJb1ZhuJoe7nnGsPa216DWRnA.png?1760634200)**

  


  


####  _**Step 2:** Create Listing_

  


  * Click **\+ New Listing** to add your first rental item or property.  
  


  * Configure its name, description, price, and availability.  
  


  * Use **Advanced Settings** to define inventory, variants, pricing, and booking rules.

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

  


**Q: How do Calendar and Appointments differ?**  
Use **Calendar View** for a visual schedule and overlaps; use **Appointments List View** for per‑listing rows, payments, and quick actions.

  


**Q: What’s the difference between Listings and Categories?**  
A **listing** is a rentable item. A **category** groups listings and influences how they’re organized on the booking page.

  


**Q: Do global changes affect existing bookings?**  
Most settings apply to **new** bookings going forward. Existing bookings and issued invoices typically remain unchanged.

  


**Q: Where do I edit pricing if totals look off?**  
Adjust rules on the listing’s **Pricing** tab (see **Pricing & Discounts**) and re‑test.

  


**Q: How do external calendars affect availability?**  
Imported events block time; exports share your availability. Use **Refresh** in a listing’s **Calendar Sync** to force an update.
