#### **Overview**  
  


Welcome to **Rentals** , our new scheduling and booking system designed for businesses that manage rentals — whether it’s cars, equipment, properties, or other bookable assets. With Rentals, you can efficiently manage availability, inventory, pricing, and multi-day bookings in one seamless platform.

  


* * *

## **What's New in Rentals**

  


Rentals is built to handle the unique complexities of rental-based scheduling. Here’s what’s Rentals can do:

  


  


  * #### **Multi-Day Booking Support:** Book assets across multiple hours, days, weeks, or months, with flexible duration and booking controls.  
  


  * #### **Listings and Categories:[(Help Guide)](<https://help.gohighlevel.com/en/support/solutions/articles/155000006573>) **Create listings for your rentals with detailed information, organized categories, and status management.  
  


  * #### **Inventory & Variants: [(Help Guide)](<https://help.gohighlevel.com/en/support/solutions/articles/155000006579>) **Enable quantity tracking for multiple units and define variants for different versions of the same listing.  
  


  * #### **Advanced Pricing & Discounts: [(Help Guide)](<https://help.gohighlevel.com/en/support/solutions/articles/155000006611>) **Use pricing rules for seasons, days of the week, hour of day, durations, or quantities. Automatically apply discounts for long-term or bulk bookings.  
  


  * #### **Calendar View:[(Help Guide)](<https://help.gohighlevel.com/en/support/solutions/articles/155000006641>) **View all bookings visually using a modern, filterable calendar that supports day, week, and month views. Identify overlaps, blocked slots, and real-time availability.  
  


  * #### **External Calendar Sync:** Connect and sync bookings with third party platforms using Calendar Sync to ensure your availability is consistent across platforms.  
  


  * #### **Centralized Global Settings:[(Help Guide)](<https://help.gohighlevel.com/en/support/solutions/articles/155000006640>) **Control business operating hours, booking defaults, payment preferences, and communication templates from one location.  
  


  * #### **Built-in Payments and Invoicing:** Collect payments directly from the booking flow and generate invoices automatically for each confirmed booking. Setup security deposits per your business needs.  
  


  * #### **Customer Booking Page:** Offer customers a streamlined booking experience with a dedicated public-facing booking page that displays your listings, categories, pricing and availability in real-time.

## **How to Enable/Disable Rentals for a Single Sub-Account****?**

  


Rentals will be enabled by default for all your new locations. However, to enable/disable it for locations, you can follow the below steps.  
  


You can enable/disable Rentals for sub-accounts directly from **Agency Settings**. Follow these steps to activate/deactivate Rentals in a single sub-account!

  


  


#### **_Step 1:_**_Navigate to Sub-Accounts_

  


  * From the Agency dashboard, select the **Sub-Accounts** tab on the left-side navigation bar  
  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155066059135/original/LIF6t_S9WvKgWm9z4B7onsUBwU6DQg6CGw.jpeg?1772524420)

  


####   


#### ** _Step 2:_**_Go to Manage Client_

  


  * Use the search bar or scroll to locate the sub-account where you would like to activate Rentals  
  

  * Click the **Three Dots (⋮)** icon in the bottom right of the sub-account panel  
  

  * Select the **Manage Client** Option  
  


#### ![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155066059137/original/3yPnvna8hxOV2RJsGLA9odPGiHq7_K6yvw.jpeg?1772524420)

  


#### ** _Step 3:_**_Turn on Rentals (Single Sub-Account)_

  


  * Select **Calendar Settings** from the top navigation bar  
  

  * Use the toggle to Turn On **Rentals**  
  

  * Click the blue **Save** button to save your changes  
  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155073934447/original/4dRLXK1IOp4P9nwUOtN8soqPZxqjQUnndw.png?1781762999)

## **How to Manage Rentals for Multiple Sub-Accounts****?**

  


You can enable/disable Rentals for sub-accounts directly from **Agency Settings**. Follow these steps to activate Rentals for multiple sub-accounts at once!

  


  


#### **_Step 1:_**_Navigate to Sub-Accounts_

  


  * From the Agency dashboard, select the **Sub-Accounts** tab on the left-side navigation bar  
  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155066059136/original/eQrOEdhRotlj_Qh9JEUVUKZWxWl_cU62Lg.jpeg?1772524420)

  


  


####  _**Step 2:** Open**** Setup Calendars_

  


  * Select the sub-accounts where you would like to activate Rentals using the checkboxes  
  

  * Once sub-accounts are selected, click **Bulk Actions** in the upper-right corner  
  

  * From the drop down menu, pick **Setup Calendars**


  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155066059139/original/DtaDFABwNTB99jODyi3on53c21Jqin-Z1g.jpeg?1772524422)

  


####  _**Step 3:**__Turn on Rentals (Multiple Sub-Accounts)_

  


  * Use the toggle to Turn On **Rentals**  
  

  * Type **CONFIRM** then click the blue **Save** button to save your changes


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155073934849/original/25FX3swjZQMGa_U5xWaE_w3D9qf-iWFY5g.png?1781763257)

## **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)**

  


**Q: How can I check if Rentals is activated for any location?**  
A: Go to **Calendars → Calendar Settings** to see if the **Rentals** tab is visible.  
  


**Q: I was a part of the beta, will my data transfer to the general release?**  
A: Yes. All data created during the beta will remain available when Rentals moves out of beta.
