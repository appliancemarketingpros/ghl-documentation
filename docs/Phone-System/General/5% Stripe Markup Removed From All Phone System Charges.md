# **What Changed**

  


HighLevel previously added a 5% markup to certain Phone System fees to offset Stripe processing costs.  
  


As of **October 1, 2025** , this markup has been permanently removed.

  


HighLevel now covers Stripe’s processing fee internally, allowing all Phone System usage charges to appear at **exact carrier passthrough cost**.

* * *

## **Key Benefits**  
  


  * **Transparent Billing** — No more confusing markup or uplifted fees.  
  


  * **Lower Usage Costs** — You pay only what the carrier charges.  
  


  * **Improved Compliance** — Passthrough telecom fees remain non-taxable.  
  


  * **Consistency Across Phone System Features** — SMS, MMS, A2P, Voice Intelligence, and Polly all follow the same passthrough billing model.


* * *

## **Messaging Charges Now at Passthrough Cost**

  


The following messaging items now reflect true carrier pricing:  
  


  * SMS carrier fees  
  


  * MMS carrier fees  
  


  * A2P 10DLC brand registration  
  


  * A2P 10DLC campaign registration  
  


  * Group messaging user fees


* * *

## **Voice Charges Now at Passthrough Cost**

  


The following voice-related services now bill at exact passthrough cost:  
  


  * Verified Caller ID  
  


  * Voice Intelligence  
  


  * Call transcription  
  


  * Amazon Polly (text-to-speech)


* * *

## **How Billing Statements Look Now**

  


Billing statements no longer include:  
  


  * A 5% uplift  
  


  * “Processing fees”  
  


  * “×1.05” multipliers


  


You will now see only the **raw carrier rate**.

## **Tax Compliance Improvements**

  


Passthrough telecommunications fees are typically non-taxable. Removing the markup ensures all Phone System usage remains categorized correctly and avoids tax-related inconsistencies across jurisdictions.

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

  


**Do I need to modify SaaS reselling prices?**

No. Your custom SaaS markups remain unchanged.

  


**Will my wallet last longer now?**

Yes. Because charges are lower, your credits deplete more slowly.

  


**Did HighLevel update past invoices?**

No. Past invoices remain unchanged. The update applies only to usage on or after **October 1, 2025**.

  


**Does this affect messaging discounts?**

No. Discounts continue to apply, now calculated against true passthrough cost.

  


**Does Auto-Recharge behavior change?**

No. Auto-Recharge continues to work the same way as before.
