# Updated Messaging Policies for Canadian 10DLC Numbers: A2P Registration Requirements

**Source URL:** [https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/155000004915-updated-messaging-policies-for-canadian-10dlc-numbers-a2p-registration-requirements](https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/155000004915-updated-messaging-policies-for-canadian-10dlc-numbers-a2p-registration-requirements)  
**Category:** Phone System  
**Folder:** A2P registration

---

This article explains when **A2P Brand & Campaign registration** (10DLC) is required for **Canadian 10DLC long code numbers** , based on where your SMS is being sent (Canada vs. United States) and when the number was purchased.

  

    
    
    **Note:** These requirements apply to **Canadian 10DLC long code numbers**. **Toll-free numbers are not affected.**

## **Key** **takeaways**

**  
**

  * **Canada → United States (CA → US):** **A2P registration is required** for **all** Canadian 10DLC numbers (new and existing).  
  


  * **Canada → Canada (CA → CA):****  
**

    * **Numbers purchased before March 26, 2025:** A2P registration is **not required** for Canada-only messaging.  
  


    * **Numbers purchased on or after March 26, 2025:** You can send Canada-only messages by completing **A2P registration** **or** **Persona verification** (if you choose not to complete A2P).


* * *

## **Rules by messaging route**

###   


### **1) Messaging from Canada to the United States (CA → US)**

  


**A2P registration is required** for sending SMS from Canadian 10DLC numbers to **U.S. recipients**.

  * Applies to **all** Canadian 10DLC numbers (new and existing).  
  


  * If you plan to message U.S. recipients, complete A2P **before** sending messages.


###   


### **2) Messaging within Canada (CA → CA)**

  


Requirements depend on **when the Canadian 10DLC number was purchased** :

  


**A) Numbers purchased before March 26, 2025**

  * A2P registration is **not required** for **Canada-only** messaging.  
  


  * If you later message U.S. recipients (CA → US), A2P registration **will be required**.


  


**B) Numbers purchased on or after March 26, 2025**

  


  * For **Canada-only** messaging, you can proceed in one of two ways:

    1. **Complete A2P registration** , **or**

    2. **Complete Persona verification** (if you choose not to complete A2P).


  


* * *

## **What you need to do**

  


Use the checklist below based on your messaging route:

  


### If you message U.S. recipients (CA → US)

  1. Complete **A2P Brand & Campaign registration** for your Canadian 10DLC number.  
  


  2. Only begin messaging U.S. recipients after your registration is approved.


###   


### If you only message Canadian recipients (CA → CA)

  * **If your number was purchased before March 26, 2025:**

    * No action required for Canada-only messaging.  
  


  * **If your number was purchased on or after March 26, 2025:**

    * Complete **A2P registration** **or** **Persona verification** before sending messages to Canadian recipients.


* * *

## **Additional details**

  


  * These requirements are based on **destination country** (where the recipient is) and the **purchase date** of the Canadian 10DLC number.  
  


  * If your business messaging needs change (e.g., you start messaging U.S. recipients), you may need to complete A2P even if you previously sent only within Canada.

## **FAQs**

###   


**Q: Do these requirements apply to toll-free numbers?**

No. These requirements apply to **Canadian 10DLC long code numbers**. **Toll-free numbers are not affected.**

###   


**Q: If I only message Canadian recipients (CA → CA), do I need A2P?**

It depends on when your Canadian 10DLC number was purchased:

  * **Purchased before March 26, 2025:** A2P registration is **not required** for Canada-only messaging.

  * **Purchased on or after March 26, 2025:** you can send Canada-only messages by completing **A2P registration** **or** **Persona verification** (if you choose not to complete A2P).


###   


**Q: If I message U.S. recipients (CA → US), can I use Persona instead of A2P?**

No. For **CA → US** messaging, **A2P registration is required** for Canadian 10DLC numbers.

###   


**Q: What happens if I start with CA → CA only and later want to message the U.S.?**

If you later message **U.S. recipients** , you must complete **A2P Brand & Campaign registration** before sending **CA → US** messages, even if you previously sent only within Canada.

###   


**Q: How do I know whether my number was purchased before or after March 26, 2025?**

Check the purchase/assignment date for the phone number in your account’s phone number management area. If you’re unsure, contact support with the number and sub-account/location details.

###   


**Q: What should I do if messages are failing?**

Confirm:

  * The destination route (CA → CA vs. CA → US)  
  


  * Whether your number requires **A2P** or **Persona** based on its purchase date  
  


  * Your registration/verification status in the Trust Center
