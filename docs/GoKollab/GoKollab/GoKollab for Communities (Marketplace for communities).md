## **Navigation & UI Update**

  
GoKollab navigation has been rebuilt to reduce friction from login through group switching. Members can move between Communities and Courses more quickly with a unified, responsive UI.

  * **Unified Login Flow:** Joined communities and courses appear in one seamless view.

  * **New Bottom Navigation Bar:** One-tap switching between Courses and Communities.

  * **UI refinements:** Improved spacing, typography, and icon clarity for smoother reading and interaction.

  * **Simplified Group Switcher:** Cleaner, quicker navigation between joined spaces.


    
    
    Tip: Update to the latest app version to experience these improvements.

## **Discover, Connect & Expand Your Reach**

  


  * Find Your People! Browsing through GoKollab has never been easier. Search for free and paid groups, explore different communities, and find your new favourite spaces to hang out.  
  

  * Create Your Own Group (No Sweat!): Want to start your own? It’s as easy as 1-2-3. Set it up in just a few clicks, and watch your new community grow.  
  

  * Smooth Signup & Snazzy Management  
  

  * Fast & Friendly Signup: Sign up with Google or your email — we won’t make you jump through hoops. Already have an account? Sign-in and you’ll be in and connecting with your community in no time.  
  

  * Manage Like a Pro: Want to update your group’s visibility? Tweak your settings? Get a custom group URL that’s easy to share? It’s all just a click away in your account settings. Control freaks, rejoice!  
  

  * Fancy New Community About Pages  
  

  * Info-packed and Eye-catching: Each group page gives you everything you need to know at a glance — member count, admin details, a killer description, and, of course, that “Join” button begging to be clicked.  
  

  * Spread the Word: Add your slick new group videos or images to get the word started on the "About Page"  
  

  * Ready to Rock? Don’t miss out on this exciting update! Activate your community on GoKollab, start discovering awesome new groups, and get your own community growing like never before.  
  

  * **Share:** Copy or edit your community URL to share it with others.

  


**Note:** GoKollab now supports case-insensitive community URLs (for example, `/Community` and `/community` both work). If you shared an older community URL before the slug migration, GoKollab redirects it to the updated URL.

  


  * SEO & Discoverability: GoKollab has improved page structure and navigation to help search engines and visitors better understand community listings. This includes clearer headings, richer page metadata, and more structured category navigation.

  


  * Head to gokollab.com/discovery and check it out!  
  

  * **Browsing shortcuts  
** Open featured listings faster: Double-click a featured community or course thumbnail to open it.  
Scroll Top sections like an app: Click-and-drag (or trackpad-drag) to move through carousels on desktop.
