Arrangement of products within the Product list page was an issue. Buyers always faced issue with the discoverability of products and as a store owner this was a bottleneck. To tackle this problem, store owners can now add the sort and filter option in the product list page.

## **Mobile Product List Page (PLP)**

  
Mobile shoppers need quick ways to narrow large catalogs on small screens. The PLP now includes touch-optimized Sort and Filter controls, a dynamic product count, and builder-side styling so you can match your brand.

  


### **Mobile Sort (Bottom Sheet)**

  


A dedicated Sort button appears in the PLP top bar on mobile. Tapping opens a bottom-sheet modal with common sort choices.

  


**Available Options**  
  


  * Date (Old → New, New → Old)  
  

  * Alphabetical (A–Z, Z–A)  
  

  * Price (Low → High, High → Low)


  


**Tips**

  


  * Dismiss the sort sheet by tapping outside or swiping down.  
  

  * Set a Default Sorting option in the widget to guide first-load ordering.


  

    
    
    Product titles on the Product List Page (PLP) continue to follow the existing truncation behavior (ellipses). Full-title wrapping applies to most other store pages, but not the PLP.

## **Mobile Filters (Full-Screen Modal)**

  


Filtering helps shoppers see only relevant items. On mobile, Filters open in a **full-screen** modal with a sticky footer for easy actions.

  


**Filter Types**  
  


  * Availability: In stock / Out of Stock  
  
  
  
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155064233595/original/49CXWnYA0OkSjj4uSentVrR-INxyiE2gcA.jpeg?1770278874)  
  

  * Price Range: From and To numeric inputs


  
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155064233666/original/0pBW_fLvRxGyLH_RAggT4WytZsjlfttujA.jpeg?1770278937)

  
**Behavior**  
  


  * Apply instantly updates results; Clear Filters resets selections.  
  

  * The Filters button shows a badge count for active filters.


  


![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155064233677/original/WXtTLxx5J6ZU_UZBVSHo6xNiNPhAketTwQ.png?1770278960)

  


Availability defaults (both selected) and price validation behavior follow store settings. If you’ve customized availability rules, only eligible products will surface.

## **How to Use:**  
**  
**

  1. Navigate to the product list page, and select the product list element. There would now be 2 toggles to enable Sorting and Filtering. Enable them to add Sort & Filter elements to your Product List page. If your store has a large catalog, you can reduce pagination on the Product Listing Page by increasing how many products display per page.  
  
  
  
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155064993331/original/4QSoYtZjvSndKsHjIcTuRe7Xcj-N0GfqUw.png?1771240152)  
  

  2. Configure properties for the sort and Filter. Some of the properties that can be configured are:


  * Sort and filter text color  
  

  * Sort and filter pill color  
  

  * Reset button color|  
  


3\. Click on save/Publish to view your changes in your site!

  
**Some Key Aspects:**  
**  
**

  * Sort and filter options are only available in the product list page  
  

  * Default sort order is Date - Old to new  
  

  * By default all products will be shown without any filters

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

**  
**

**Q1: Where do I enable mobile sorting and filtering?**  
Enable both in the Product List widget → General tab → toggle Enable sorting and Enable filtering.

  


**Q2: Can shoppers change the sort order on mobile?  
** Yes. Tapping Sort opens a bottom-sheet with Date, Alphabetical, and Price options (each with two directions).

  


**Q3: What filters are available on mobile?  
** Availability (In stock / Out of Stock) and Price Range (From/To). The Filters modal is full-screen with a sticky Apply/Clear footer.

  


**Q4: Are Availability options selected by default?  
** By default both are selected so shoppers see all products until they refine. (If your store theme overrides defaults, behavior follows store settings.)

  


**Q5: Does the PLP show how many products match?  
** Yes. A product count appears at the top and updates dynamically when filters change.

  


**Q6: What does the badge on the Filters button mean?  
** It shows how many filter criteria are currently active.
