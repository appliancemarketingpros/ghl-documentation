# **What is the Built-In Image Editor in Media Library?**

  


The Built-In Image Editor is a lightweight, on-screen workspace embedded in the Media Library. It focuses on essential adjustments that marketers and teams routinely need, helping you prep images right before publishing without downloading files or opening third‑party apps. The editor opens from any image in the Media Library after you click Edit, rendering a modal with tools for crop, rotate, flip, brightness, and contrast, plus undo/redo and save options.

* * *

## **Key Benefits of the Built-In Image Editor**

  


  * **Work Inside HighLevel:** Eliminate external tools and context switching.  
  

  * **Cleaner Asset Management:** Replace or save as new to prevent duplicate clutter.  
  

  * **On-brand Visuals:** Adjust right before publishing to maintain consistency.  
  

  * **Lower Storage Overhead:** Avoid multiple near-duplicate uploads.  
  

  * **Faster multi-module workflows:** Edited images are immediately available anywhere the Media Library appears (e.g., Social Planner, Funnels, Email Builder).

## **How To Use the Built-In Image Editor**

  


Follow these steps to edit from any supported module:

  


### _**Step 1:** Open the Edit Modal_

  


  1. Click on **Media Storage** from your sub-account.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/WQSPYy6VbJaPDVQauwKg+/Screen+Shot+2025-10-06+at+9.36.25+PM.png)  
  

  2. Or open any Media Library supported module like Social Planner and click on **Upload from Media Storage.**  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/Xf50G226djyrWgmblP82+/GIF+Recording+2025-10-06+at+9.38.12+PM.gif)  
  

  3. Hover on the image you want to edit and click on the **three dots** on the top right corner.  
  

  4. Click on **Edit**.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/FnB9mNqX9qfhalAbfLmZ+/Screen+Shot+2025-10-06+at+9.42.33+PM.png)  
  


### _**Step 2:** Edit an Image_

  


  1. **Crop** an image using custom aspect ratio or pre-set aspect ratios.  
  

  2. You can also **flip** an **image** **horizontally** or **vertically**.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/WL24n4mgzhDQSyxwVH4Y+/GIF+Recording+2025-10-06+at+9.47.13+PM.gif)  
  

  3. Apply **Filters** to the image from a wide range of options.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/tGOTI3l9qz6FycCv5NHV+/GIF+Recording+2025-10-06+at+10.01.08+PM.gif)  
  

  4. Make **Adjustments** to the image like **brightness** , **contrast** , **saturation** , and **blur**.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/PKfoPxSsFNAk4b4OFYf5+/GIF+Recording+2025-10-06+at+10.03.23+PM.gif)  
  

  5. Add **Text** to the image and customize the **Font** **Family** , **Font** **Size** , **Alignment** , **Font** **Color** , **Background** **color** , and **Line** **Spacing**.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/FG5ghofe7vfFYV5UpYj3+/GIF+Recording+2025-10-06+at+10.18.45+PM.gif)

![](https://jumpshare.com/share/GR2tDaKP2JcHaiyJMP2e+/GIF+Recording+2025-10-06+at+11.01.39+PM.gif)

![](https://jumpshare.com/share/sTT4GdCawsZcRlzeoW0c+/Screen+Shot+2025-10-06+at+11.08.21+PM.png)  
  

  9. Click on **Save** , **name** **the** **image** and you can also choose to **Save** **to** **Folder**.  
  

  10. Click on **Save** to store it to the Media Library as a new image.  
  
![](https://jumpshare.com/share/6E7Sc6l6Tb1mJXGTrEUZ+/GIF+Recording+2025-10-06+at+11.10.49+PM.gif)

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

  


  


**Q: Is the editor available in the mobile app?  
** Not yet. The feature currently targets desktop. Edits made on desktop are visible on mobile once saved.

  


**Q: Which file types can I edit?  
**

Supported image formats are PNG and JPEG.

  


**Q: Can I bulk edit multiple images at once?  
** Not currently. Open and edit images one at a time.
