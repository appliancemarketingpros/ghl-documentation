## **Overview**

This guide will help you set up and customize your iOS white-label mobile app inside **Apple App Store Connect**. Follow the steps carefully to ensure smooth submission and publishing.

* * *

## **Prerequisites**

  * An **active Apple Developer Account**
  * Your **App Store Connect login credentials**
  * Prepared assets:
    * App Name & Subtitle
    * App Icon (1024x1024 PNG, no transparency)
    * Screenshots for iPhone & iPad
    * Privacy Policy & Support URLs
    * Marketing Description & Keywords


* * *

## **Step by step: Create the app in App Store Connect**

## **Video tutorial** : [https://app.arcade.software/flows/gaZgnDZCdU3G2BF9xpk1/view](<https://app.arcade.software/flows/gaZgnDZCdU3G2BF9xpk1/view>)  
  
**Step 1: Log in to App Store Connect**

  1. Go to [App Store Connect](<https://appstoreconnect.apple.com/>).
  2. Sign in with your Apple Developer credentials.
  3. From the **Apps** dashboard, click the ➕ icon to create a new app, or select your existing white-label app to update.


* * *

## **Step 2: Enter App Information**

  * **App Name** – Enter your brand’s app name.
  * **Subtitle** – A short tagline (max 30 characters).
  * **Primary Language** – Usually English (U.S.) unless your app targets another language.
  * **Bundle ID** – Select the correct bundle ID (must match what was created in Xcode/Certificates).
  * **Category** – Choose your app category (e.g., Productivity, Business).
  * **SKU** – Unique identifier (can be your brand or project code).


* * *

## **Step 3: Add Branding Assets**

  1. Upload your **App Icon** (1024x1024, PNG, no rounded corners).
  2. Upload **Screenshots** for different iPhone/iPad screen sizes:
     * 6.9", 6.5", 5.5" displays (minimum 3 screenshots per size).
     * Ensure screenshots represent the app’s user experience.


* * *

## **Step 4: Add App Metadata**

  * **Description** – A detailed explanation of your app’s purpose and features.
  * **Keywords** – Add relevant keywords for discoverability.
  * **Promotional Text** – Optional, appears at the top of your App Store page.
  * **Support URL** – A valid link for customer support.
  * **Marketing URL** – (Optional) Link to your app’s website/landing page.
  * **Copyright Holder** – Your company or brand name.


* * *

## **Step 5: Configure App Privacy**

  1. Provide your **Privacy Policy URL**.
  2. Declare what **data is collected and linked to users** (e.g., location, identifiers, diagnostics).
  3. Review “Data Linked to You” and “Data Not Linked to You” sections.


* * *

## **Step 6: Pricing & Availability**

  * Choose your **Price Schedule** (Free or Paid).
  * Set your **Base Country or Region** (e.g., United States).
  * Select availability across **175 countries/regions**.


* * *

## **Step 7: App Review Information**

  * If your app requires login, provide **test account credentials** (demo username & password).
  * Add **contact details** (name, phone, email).
  * If special permissions (like VoIP, background audio) are required, explain them in the **Notes** section.


* * *

## **Step 8: Submit for Review**

  1. Verify all required fields are filled.
  2. Ensure no errors (like missing app icon, incorrect description, or unsupported characters like emojis).
  3. Click **Submit for Review**.
  4. Apple will review and approve before your app goes live.


* * *

## **⚠️ Common Issues to Avoid**

  * ❌ Missing App Store icon or incorrect size
  * ❌ Emojis or unsupported characters in description
  * ❌ Phone number without country code
  * ❌ Incorrect locale (should be `en-US` for U.S.)
  * ❌ Not enabling required APIs in case of Google Play builds
