# How to Customize Your White Label HighLevel Mobile App

**Source URL:** [https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/155000005009-how-to-customize-your-white-label-highlevel-mobile-app](https://help.gohighlevel.com/support/solutions/articles/155000005009-how-to-customize-your-white-label-highlevel-mobile-app)  
**Category:** Mobile & Desktop App  
**Folder:** Whitelabel Mobile App

---

You can customize your mobile app’s branding, layout, and navigation directly from the **Mobile App Customiser**

##   


## **Step 1: Access Customization**

  * Go to **Agency View → Mobile App**

  * Click on **“Customise App”**


This will open the **App customisation**

* * *

## **Step 2: App Customization → Branding**

### 2.1 **Essential Branding**

Update your app’s core identity:

  * **App Name**

  * **App Logo**

  * **Primary Color**

  * **Font**


Click **Save and Next** after making changes.

* * *

### 2.2 **Splash Screen**

Customize the app launch screen:

  * Upload **Light Mode Splash Screen**

  * Upload **Dark Mode Splash Screen**


Click **Save and Next** to proceed.

* * *

## **Step 3: App Customization → Customization**

### 3.1 **Login Screen**

Customize the login experience:

  * Login screen logo (Light & Dark mode)

  * Title text

  * Subtitle text

  * CTA button text


Click **Save and Next**

* * *

### 3.2 **Core Navigation**

Control the main navigation of the app:

#### Top Navigation Bar

  * Add or edit items (e.g., Notifications, Profile)


#### Bottom Navigation Bar

  * Manage visible tabs (e.g., Dashboard, Conversations, Apps)

  * Reorder or remove modules


Note: There is a limit on the number of modules you can add.

Click **Save and Next**

* * *

### 3.3 **App Drawer**

Customize the full app menu:

  * Add, remove, or reorder modules

  * Organize apps into categories


* * *

## **Step 4: Add Custom Module** (Optional)

You can add custom functionality using a module:

Click **“Add More”** → Fill in:

  * **Module Title**

  * **Module URL**

  * **Select Icon**

  * Choose behavior:

    * Open inside app

    * Open in mobile browser

  * Assign access:

    * Select users/accounts

    * Role-based visibility


Click **Save**

* * *

## **Final Step: Save Changes**

  * Click **Save** to apply all customizations

  * Ensure all changes are properly saved before generating a new build

  * Start a new build to [push updates](<https://help.gohighlevel.com/a/solutions/articles/155000007242?portalId=48000045315>) for new customisations 


* * *

## Best Practice

  * Complete all customization steps before updating the app

  * Review preview screens to ensure correct layout and branding

  * Avoid incomplete configurations to prevent issues in the live app
